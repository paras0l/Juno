# 同位语从句

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 32 |
| 语法点 | 同位语从句 |
| 核心内容 | 对抽象名词进行解释 |

## 常用词组/搭配

fact that, idea that, news that, hope that

## 详细笔记

### 一、同位语从句定义

- **定义**：对前面抽象名词进行解释说明的从句称为同位语从句
- **位置**：位于某些抽象名词（如 fact, idea, news, hope, problem 等）之后

### 二、常跟同位语从句的名词

| 名词 | 用法 |
|------|------|
| fact | The fact that he is honest is true. |
| idea | I have an idea that we should go there. |
| news | The news that he won made us happy. |
| hope | His hope that he would pass came true. |
| problem | The problem that we face is money. |
| question | The question whether we can go is unclear. |
| belief | His belief that everyone is equal is strong. |
| doubt | There is no doubt that he is right. |

### 三、同位语从句的引导词

#### 1. 从属连词 that
- The fact **that** he is honest is true.
- The news **that** she won made us happy.

#### 2. 从属连词 whether
- The question **whether** we can win is unclear.
- There is no doubt **whether** he will come.

#### 3. 疑问词
- The question **how** we can solve it is important.
- I have no idea **what** we should do.

### 四、同位语从句的位置

#### 1. 紧跟在名词后
- **The fact that** he is honest is true.

#### 2. 用 it 作形式主语（少数情况）
- **It is a fact that** he is honest.

### 五、同位语从句与定语从句的区别

| 特征 | 同位语从句 | 定语从句 |
|------|------------|----------|
| **作用** | 解释说明抽象名词的含义 | 修饰限定名词 |
| **性质** | 从句是名词的内容 | 从句是名词的修饰语 |
| **引导词** | that/whether/疑问词 | that/which/who/whom/whose/where/when/why |
| **例句** | The news **that** he came is true. | The news **that** he told me is true. |
| **翻译** | 他来了这个消息（是）真的 | 他告诉我的那个消息（是）真的 |

### 六、同位语从句的简化

- 同位语从句可简化为不定式
- The hope **to win** came true. = The hope **that** he **would win** came true.
- The decision **to go** was made. = The decision **that** we **would go** was made.

### 例句

#### that 引导
1. The fact **that** he is honest is true.
2. The news **that** she won the competition made us happy.
3. The idea **that** we should work together is good.
4. I have a belief **that** everyone can succeed.

#### whether 引导
1. The question **whether** we can finish on time is unclear.
2. There is no doubt **whether** he will come.
3. The problem **whether** we should accept the offer remains.

#### 疑问词引导
1. The question **how** we can solve this problem is important.
2. I have no idea **what** we should do next.
3. Please answer the question **when** we will have the meeting.

#### 同位语从句 vs. 定语从句
1. The news **that** he came is true. （同位语，解释 news 内容）
2. The news **that** he told me is interesting. （定语，修饰 news）

### 注意事项

1. **同位语从句的 that 不能省略**：
   - The fact that he is honest is true.

2. **同位语从句用 whether，不用 if**：
   - The question whether we can go is not decided.

3. **区分同位语从句和定语从句**：
   - 同位语从句：从句是名词的内容/解释
   - 定语从句：从句是名词的修饰语/限定

4. **同位语从句可简化为不定式**：
   - The hope to succeed is strong. = The hope that he would succeed is strong.

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的引导词填空

1. The fact ____ he is honest is true.
2. The news ____ she won made us happy.
3. The question ____ we can go is not decided.
4. The problem ____ we should do next is important.
5. I have no idea ____ we will do.

#### 二、选择题

1. The news ____ he won made us happy.
   A. that B. which C. what D. when

2. The question ____ we can finish on time is unclear.
   A. that B. if C. whether D. which

3. The fact ____ he is right is clear.
   A. that B. which C. what D. whether

4. I have no idea ____ he will come.
   A. that B. if C. whether D. what

5. The idea ____ we should try again is good.
   A. that B. which C. what D. how

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. He is honest. The fact is true. （用同位语从句合并）
   _________________________________________________
2. We can finish the work. The question is not clear. （用同位语从句合并）
   _________________________________________________
3. The news is true. He came here. （用同位语从句合并）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. that    2. that    3. whether    4. what    5. whether/if

**二、**
1. A（that 引导同位语从句）
2. C（whether 表是否）
3. A（that 引导同位语从句）
4. C（whether/if 表是否）
5. A（that 引导同位语从句）

**三、**
1. The fact that he is honest is true.
2. The question whether we can finish the work is not clear.
3. The news that he came here is true.
