## 详细笔记

### 一、关系副词概述

- **定义**：在定语从句中代替先行词，表示时间、地点、原因、目的等
- **形式**：when（时间）, where（地点）, why（原因）

### 二、关系副词详解

#### 1. when（时间）
- **先行词**：time, day, year, moment 等
- **在从句中作时间状语**
- This is the day **when** he was born. = This is the day **on which** he was born.
- I still remember the time **when** we were together.

#### 2. where（地点）
- **先行词**：place, city, country, house 等
- **在从句中作地点状语**
- This is the place **where** we met. = This is the place **in which** we met.
- Beijing is the city **where** I grew up.

#### 3. why（原因）
- **先行词**：reason
- **在从句中作原因状语**
- That's the reason **why** I was late. = That's the reason **for which** I was late.
- Please explain the reason **why** you are absent.

### 三、关系副词 vs. 介词 + which

| 关系副词 | 相当于介词 + which |
|----------|-------------------|
| when = on/in which | 时间 |
| where = in/at/on which | 地点 |
| why = for which | 原因 |

- The day **when** he came = The day **on which** he came
- The place **where** we met = The place **in which** we met
- The reason **why** I left = The reason **for which** I left

### 四、关系副词在从句中可省略

- 先行词明确时，关系副词可省略
- This is the place (where) we met last year.
- That's the reason (why) I came here.

### 五、抽象先行词

- 某些抽象名词后也可用关系副词
- This is the point **where** we disagree. （分歧点）
- This is the stage **where** we are now. （阶段）
- That's the way **how** he solves problems. （方式）

### 六、难点：when/where/why vs. that/which

| 先行词 | 用关系副词 | 用介词+which |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| time | when | on which |
| place | where | in which |
| reason | why | for which |

- I remember the day **when** I met her. ✓
- I remember the day **on which** I met her. ✓
- I remember the day **which** I met her on. （口语）

### 例句

#### when 引导
1. This is the day **when** he was born.
2. I still remember the time **when** we first met.
3. Do you remember the year **when** we graduated?

#### where 引导
1. This is the place **where** we first met.
2. That's the house **where** I grew up.
3. Shanghai is the city **where** I work.

#### why 引导
1. That's the reason **why** I was late.
2. Please tell me the reason **why** you are absent.
3. Nobody understands the reason **why** he left.

#### 关系副词 = 介词 + which
1. The day **when** we met = The day **on which** we met.
2. The place **where** we work = The place **in which** we work.
3. The reason **why** I came = The reason **for which** I came.

#### 省略关系副词
1. This is the place (where) we met last year.
2. That's the reason (why) I came here.
3. I'll never forget the day (when) we got married.

### 注意事项

1. **关系副词不能作主语或宾语**，只能作状语。
   - 错误：This is the book when I bought.
   - 正确：This is the book (which/that) I bought. / This is when I bought the book.

2. **when/where/why 可用 that 替代**（口语中）：
   - The reason (why/that) I came is clear.

3. **关系副词可转换为介词 + which**：
   - when = on which
   - where = in which/at which
   - why = for which

4. **先行词是 time/day/year 时**，关系副词 when 更常用。

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的关系副词填空

1. This is the place ____ we met last year.
2. That's the reason ____ I was late.
3. I still remember the day ____ we first met.
4. Shanghai is the city ____ I work.
5. Please tell me the reason ____ you are absent.

#### 二、选择题

1. This is the reason ____ I came here.
   A. which B. why C. when D. where

2. I'll never forget the year ____ we got married.
   A. which B. why C. when D. where

3. This is the house ____ I grew up.
   A. which B. why C. when D. where

4. The reason ____ he failed is that he didn't study.
   A. which B. why C. when D. where

5. This is the place ____ we can relax.
   A. which B. why C. when D. where
