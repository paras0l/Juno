# 宾语从句

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 29 |
| 语法点 | 宾语从句 |
| 核心内容 | 及物动词/介词后的从句 |

## 常用词组/搭配

think/believe/say/hope that..., depend on whether

## 详细笔记

### 一、宾语从句定义

- **定义**：在句子中作宾语的从句称为宾语从句
- **位置**：位于及物动词或介词之后

### 二、宾语从句的引导词

#### 1. 从属连词
| 引导词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|--------|------|------|
| that | 无意义，仅起连接作用（可省略） | I think **that** he is right. |
| whether/if | 是否 | I don't know **whether/if** he will come. |

#### 2. 疑问代词
| 引导词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|--------|------|------|
| who | 谁（主格） | I wonder **who** she is. |
| whom | 谁（宾格） | I wonder **whom** you are waiting for. |
| what | 什么 | Tell me **what** you want. |
| which | 哪个 | I don't know **which** one to choose. |
| whose | 谁的 | I want to know **whose** book this is. |

#### 3. 疑问副词
| 引导词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|--------|------|------|
| when | 什么时候 | Please tell me **when** you will leave. |
| where | 哪里 | I don't know **where** he lives. |
| why | 为什么 | I wonder **why** she is late. |
| how | 怎样 | Tell me **how** you did it. |

### 三、宾语从句的时态

#### 1. 主句与从句时态一致的情况
- 主句现在时 → 从句可用任何时态
- I think **that** he **is** a good student.
- I think **that** he **was** here yesterday.
- I think **that** he **will come** tomorrow.

#### 2. 时态需要变化的情况
- 主句过去时 → 从句时态往回跳一步
- She said **that** she **was** a teacher. （现在 → 过去）
- He said **that** he **had finished** his work. （现在完成 → 过去完成）
- They thought **that** he **would** come. （将来 → 过去将来）

#### 3. 客观真理例外
- The teacher told us **that** the earth **goes** around the sun. （不变）

### 四、宾语从句的否定转移

- 主句主语是第一人称，且谓语是 think/believe/expect/suppse 时，从句的否定转移到主句
- I don't think **he is right**. （我认为他不对）
- I don't believe **she will come**. （我相信她不会来）

### 五、宾语从句的简化

#### 1. 简化为不定式
- Please tell me **when to leave**. = Please tell me **when I should leave**.
- I don't know **what to do**. = I don't know **what I should do**.

#### 2. 简化为"疑问词 + 不定式"结构
- 适用于 know, wonder, decide, remember, learn 等动词

### 六、介词后的宾语从句

- I am interested in **what** he said.
- It depends on **whether** we can finish it on time.
- This is the book **that** I bought yesterday. （定语从句，不是宾语从句）

### 例句

#### that 引导（可省略）
1. I think **that** he is honest.
2. She said **that** she would come.
3. I believe **(that)** you are right.

#### whether/if 引导
1. I don't know **whether/if** she will come.
2. Please ask **if** he is at home.
3. **Whether** we will go is not decided yet.

#### 疑问代词引导
1. I wonder **who** is coming.
2. Tell me **what** you need.
3. Please decide **which** one to choose.
4. I don't know **whose** book this is.

#### 疑问副词引导
1. Please tell me **when** the train leaves.
2. I want to know **where** he lives.
3. Can you explain **why** you are late?
4. Show me **how** you did it.

#### 时态变化
1. She said she **was** a teacher. （现在→过去）
2. He told me he **had finished** the work. （现在完成→过去完成）
3. They thought he **would** come. （将来→过去将来）

#### 否定转移
1. I don't think he **is** right. （我认为他不对）
2. I don't believe she **will** come. （我相信她不会来）
3. I don't suppose he **knows** the truth. （我认为他不知道）

#### 简化
1. I don't know what **to do**. = what I should do
2. Please tell me how **to use** it. = how I should use it
3. Tell me when **to start**. = when I should start

### 注意事项

1. **that 可省略**：在 think/believe/say 等动词后，that 常省略。
   - I think **(that)** he is right.

2. **if vs. whether**：
   - 两者都可表示"是否"的情况
   - 只能用 whether：介词后、不定式前、名词后
   - 只能用 if：口语中、否定句中
   - 例：I don't know **if** he will come. / It depends on **whether** we can go.

3. **否定转移的条件**：
   - 主句主语必须是第一人称
   - 谓语必须是 think/believe/expect/suppose/imagine 等
   - 否定转移后，从句用肯定形式

4. **宾语从句的语序**：必须是陈述语序（疑问词 + 主语 + 动词）。
   - 错误：I wonder **where is he**.
   - 正确：I wonder **where he is**.

5. **时态变化**：主句过去时，从句时态要"往回跳一步"。

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的引导词填空

1. I don't know ____ she will come.
2. Tell me ____ you want.
3. I wonder ____ he is waiting for.
4. Please explain ____ he was late.
5. I don't know ____ to do next.

#### 二、选择题

1. I think ____ he is right.
   A. that B. if C. what D. whether

2. I don't know ____ to choose.
   A. what B. which C. whose D. whoever

3. She said she ____ a teacher.
   A. is B. was C. be D. will be

4. I don't believe he ____ the truth.
   A. knows B. knew C. know D. knowing

5. Please tell me ____ the train leaves.
   A. when B. where C. why D. what

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. I think he is right. （改为否定句）
   _________________________________________________
2. I want to know. When will he come? （合并为宾语从句）
   _________________________________________________
3. Is she at home? I don't know. （合并为宾语从句）
   _________________________________________________
4. I don't know. What should I do? （用不定式改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. whether/if    2. what    3. whom    4. why    5. what

**二、**
1. A（that 可省略）
2. B（which one 选择）
3. B（时态回跳）
4. A（否定转移后，从句肯定）
5. A（when 引导宾语从句）

**三、**
1. I don't think he is right.
2. I want to know when he will come.
3. I don't know if/whether she is at home.
4. I don't know what to do.
