# 表语从句

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 31 |
| 语法点 | 表语从句 |
| 核心内容 | be动词后的从句 |

## 常用词组/搭配

The reason is that..., That's why...

## 详细笔记

### 一、表语从句定义

- **定义**：在句子中作表语的从句称为表语从句
- **位置**：位于系动词（be动词）之后

### 二、表语从句的引导词

#### 1. 从属连词 that / whether
- The fact **is that** he is right. （that 无意义，可省略）
- The question **is whether** we can finish it on time.

#### 2. 疑问代词
- The problem **is what** we should do next.
- This **is who** I am looking for.
- The book **is which** one you need.

#### 3. 疑问副词
- The time **is when** we should start.
- The place **is where** we should meet.
- The reason **is why** he was late.
- The way **is how** we can solve it.

#### 4. 关系副词（as if / because）
- It looks **as if** it is going to rain.
- The reason **is because** he was ill.

### 三、常见系动词后的表语从句

#### 1. be 动词
- The truth **is that** he is honest.
- The problem **is that** we have no money.

#### 2. become
- The problem **has become what** we expected.
- He **became who** he wanted to be.

#### 3. look / seem / appear
- It **looks as if** it will rain.
- He **seems (that)** he is tired.
- It **appears that** something is wrong.

#### 4. remain / stay / keep
- The question **remains whether** we can win.
- The fact **remains that** we tried our best.

### 四、常见表语从句结构

#### 1. The reason is that...
- **The reason** he was late **is that** he missed the bus.

#### 2. That's why...
- He studied hard. **That's why** he passed the exam.

#### 3. That's because...
- He didn't come. **That's because** he was ill.

#### 4. The point is...
- **The point is whether** we should accept the offer.

#### 5. It looks as if/as though...
- **It looks as if** it is going to snow.

### 五、表语从句与主语从句的区别

- 主语从句：位于句首或用 it 作形式主语
- 表语从句：位于系动词之后
- The problem is what we should do. （表语从句）
- What we should do is the problem. （主语从句）

### 六、名词性从句总结

| 从句类型 | 位置 | 例句 |
|----------|------|------|
| 主语从句 | 句首/it作形式主语 | **That** he is here is important. |
| 宾语从句 | 动词/介词后 | I know **that** he is right. |
| 表语从句 | 系动词后 | The problem **is that** we have no time. |
| 同位语从句 | 名词后 | The fact **that** he is honest is true. |

### 例句

#### that 引导
1. The fact **is that** he is honest.
2. The problem **is that** we don't have enough time.
3. My idea **is that** we should start early.

#### whether 引导
1. The question **is whether** we can finish on time.
2. The problem **is whether** we should accept his offer.

#### 疑问词引导
1. The problem **is what** we should do next.
2. This **is where** we should meet.
3. The reason **is why** he was late.
4. This **is how** we solve the problem.

#### as if/as though 引导
1. It **looks as if** it is going to rain.
2. He **seems as if** he knows the answer.
3. It **appears as though** she is not interested.

#### 常见结构
1. **The reason** he was late **is that** he missed the bus.
2. **That's why** he failed the exam.
3. He didn't come. **That's because** he was ill.
4. **The point is whether** we should agree.

### 注意事项

1. **表语从句的引导词 that 可省略**：
   - The fact **is (that)** he is honest.

2. **表语从句的语序**：用陈述语序，不用疑问语序。
   - 错误：The question is when will we leave.
   - 正确：The question is when we will leave.

3. **That's why vs. That's because**：
   - that's why：结果是……（强调原因导致的结果）
   - that's because：原因是……（强调原因）
   - He was late. That's why he missed the meeting.
   - He was late. That's because the traffic was bad.

4. **as if/as though 后可用虚拟语气**：
   - He talks as if he knew everything. （与现在事实相反）
   - He talks as if he had known everything. （与过去事实相反）

5. **表语从句不能用 if 引导**，只能用 whether。

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的引导词填空

1. The problem is ____ we don't have enough time.
2. The question is ____ we can finish the work on time.
3. This is ____ we should meet.
4. The reason is ____ he was ill.
5. It looks ____ it is going to rain.

#### 二、选择题

1. The reason he was late is ____ he missed the bus.
   A. because B. why C. that D. what

2. The question is ____ we should accept the offer.
   A. that B. which C. whether D. what

3. It looks ____ it will snow.
   A. that B. as if C. because D. why

4. This is ____ we should do next.
   A. that B. what C. which D. when

5. That's ____ he failed the exam.
   A. because B. why C. what D. how

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. The problem is: What should we do next? （改为表语从句）
   _________________________________________________
2. He studied hard. That's why he passed. （用句子合并）
   _________________________________________________
3. He was ill. That's why he didn't come. （用 that's because 改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. that    2. whether    3. where    4. why    5. as if / like

**二、**
1. A（the reason is because...）
2. C（whether 表是否）
3. B（as if 表看起来像）
4. B（what 表什么）
5. B（that's why 结果是）

**三、**
1. The problem is what we should do next.
2. That's why he studied hard and passed the exam.
3. He was ill, that's why he didn't come. / That's because he was ill that he didn't come.
