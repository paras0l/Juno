## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|

## 常用词组/搭配

the one who, the place where, the reason why

## 详细笔记

### 一、定语从句定义

- **定义**：在句子中作定语，修饰名词或代词的从句
- **位置**：位于被修饰词（先行词）之后
- **结构**：先行词 + 关系代词/关系副词 + 从句

### 二、关系代词

| 关系代词 | 先行词 | 在从句中的成分 | 例句 |
|----------|--------|----------------|------|
| who | 人 | 主语/宾语 | The man **who** came here is my friend. |
| whom | 人 | 宾语 | The man **whom** I saw yesterday is my friend. |
| which | 物/事 | 主语/宾语 | The book **which** I bought is interesting. |
| that | 人/物 | 主语/宾语 | The book **that** I read is good. |
| whose | 人/物 | 定语（所有格） | The boy **whose** name is Tom is smart. |

### 三、关系代词的选择

#### 1. 先行词指人

| 情况 | 用关系代词 |
|------|------------|
| 主语 | who / that |
| 宾语 | who / whom / that |
| 所有格 | whose |

#### 2. 先行词指物

| 情况 | 用关系代词 |
|------|------------|
| 主语 | which / that |
| 宾语 | which / that |
| 所有格 | whose / of which |

### 四、关系代词在从句中的省略

- **作宾语时**，关系代词可以省略
- The book **(which/that)** I bought is interesting.
- The man **(whom/who/that)** I saw is here.

### 五、关系代词前介词的使用

- The man **to whom** I spoke is my teacher. （whom 前置）
- The man **who(m)** I spoke to is my teacher. （省略关系代词）

### 六、that vs. which 的区别

#### 1. 只能用 that 的情况
- 先行词是 all, everything, something, anything, nothing, few, one, the only 等
- The only thing **that** matters is health.
- All **that** glitters is not gold.

#### 2. 只能用 which 的情况
- 非限制性定语从句（用逗号隔开）
- The book, **which** I bought yesterday, is interesting.
- 先行词本身是 that
- The word **that** is used here is wrong.

### 七、whose 的用法

- Whose = of which / of whom
- The boy **whose** father is a teacher is my classmate.
- The book **the cover of which** is red is mine. = The book **whose** cover is red is mine.

### 八、who vs. whom

- who：主格，在从句中作主语
- whom：宾格，在从句中作宾语
- 口语中 who 可代替 whom
- The man **who(m)** I talked to is my teacher.

### 例句

#### who 引导（指人，主语）
1. The man **who** came here is my teacher.
2. The girl **who** is singing is my sister.
3. Anyone **who** wants to go can join us.

#### whom 引导（指人，宾语）
1. The man **whom** I saw yesterday is my friend.
2. The woman **whom** he married is very kind.
3. The people **whom** we invited are coming tonight.

#### which 引导（指物，主语/宾语）
1. The book **which** I bought is interesting.
2. The book **which** is on the table is mine.
3. The computer **which** I use is new.

#### that 引导（指人/物）
1. The man **that** I saw is my brother.
2. The book **that** I read is very good.
3. The only thing **that** matters is health.

#### whose 引导（所有格）
1. The boy **whose** father is a doctor is my classmate.
2. The house **whose** windows are broken is empty.
3. I have a friend **whose** mother is a teacher.

#### 关系代词省略
1. The book **I bought** is interesting. （省略了 which/that）
2. The man **I saw** is my friend. （省略了 whom/who/that）
3. The girl **he loves** is beautiful. （省略了 whom/that）

#### 介词 + whom/which
1. The man **to whom** I spoke is my teacher.
2. The book **on which** I spent much money is good.
3. The friend **with whom** I work is kind.

### 注意事项

1. **who vs. whom**：
   - who 在从句中作主语，whom 作宾语
   - 口语中 whom 可用 who 代替，但介词后仍用 whom

2. **关系代词作宾语时可以省略**：
   - The book (which/that) I bought is good.

3. **that vs. which**：
   - 只能用 that：先行词是 all, everything, only, the only 等
   - 只能用 which：非限制性定语从句（用逗号隔开）

4. **whose 的位置**：whose 后直接接名词，不能单独使用。

5. **介词 + which/whom**：介词可提前到关系代词前。
   - The man with whom I work = The man (whom/who) I work with

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的关系代词填空

1. The man ____ came here is my teacher.
2. The book ____ I bought is interesting.
3. The girl ____ mother is a doctor is my friend.
4. The man ____ I saw yesterday is my brother.
5. I know the person ____ name is Tom.

#### 二、选择题

1. The book ____ I bought is very interesting.
   A. that B. who C. whom D. whose

2. The man ____ is speaking is my teacher.
   A. which B. who C. whom D. whose

3. The boy ____ father is a doctor is my classmate.
   A. which B. who C. whom D. whose

4. I know the girl ____ mother is a teacher.
   A. that B. who C. whom D. whose

5. This is the only thing ____ matters.
   A. which B. who C. whom D. that
