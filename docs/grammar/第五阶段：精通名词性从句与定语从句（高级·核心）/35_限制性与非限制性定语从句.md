# 限制性与非限制性定语从句

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 35 |
| 语法点 | 限制性与非限制性定语从句 |
| 核心内容 | 有无逗号的区别 |

## 常用词组/搭配

which引导整句, as is known to all

## 详细笔记

### 一、限制性定语从句

#### 1. 定义
- 限制性定语从句是先行词不可或缺的修饰成分
- 没有它，先行词的意义不完整
- **不使用逗号**与主句分隔

#### 2. 特点
- 与先行词关系密切
- 删除后会改变句子含义
- 不能省略

#### 3. 例句
1. The man **who** helped me is my teacher. （帮助我的那个人是我的老师）
2. The book **that** I bought is interesting. （我买的那本书有趣）
3. Students **who** study hard will pass. （努力学习的学生会通过）

### 二、非限制性定语从句

#### 1. 定义
- 对先行词进行补充说明
- 删除后不影响主句含义
- **使用逗号**与主句分隔

#### 2. 特点
- 与先行词关系较松散
- 只是补充信息
- 可以省略

#### 3. 例句
1. My brother, **who** lives in Shanghai, is a doctor. （我哥哥住在上海，他是医生）
2. The book, **which** cost me 100 yuan, is very good. （这本书花了我100元，很好）
3. I gave him a book, **which** he liked very much. （我给了他一本书，他很喜欢）

### 三、两者的区别

| 特征 | 限制性定语从句 | 非限制性定语从句 |
|------|----------------|------------------|
| 逗号 | 无逗号 | 有逗号 |
| 关系词 | that/who/which | 只能用 who/which |
| 省略 | 不可省略 | 可以省略 |
| 翻译 | 译为"……的" | 译为并列句 |
| 先行词 | 泛指/特指均可 | 往往是特指 |

### 四、只能用 who 的情况

- 先行词指人，且非限制性定语从句中
- My father, **who** is over 70, is still healthy.
- 不能用 that 代替 who

### 五、which 引导的非限制性定语从句

#### 1. which 代替整个主句
- He didn't come to the meeting, **which** surprised everyone. （他没有来，这让所有人惊讶）
- She was ill, **which** is why she didn't come. （她生病了，这就是她没来的原因）

#### 2. as 引导的非限制性定语从句
- **As is known to all**, China is a developing country. （众所周知，中国是发展中国家）
- He is a good student, **as we all know**. （他是好学生，这点我们都知道）

### 六、常见结构

| 结构 | 用法 |
|------|------|
| which is why | 这就是为什么 |
| which is because | 这是因为 |
| as is known | 众所周知 |
| as is said | 如上所说 |
| as is reported | 据报道 |

### 例句

#### 限制性定语从句
1. The man **who** helped me is my teacher.
2. The book **that** I bought is interesting.
3. Students **who** study hard will pass the exam.
4. The girl **whose** mother is a doctor is my friend.
5. I know the reason **why** he was late.

#### 非限制性定语从句
1. My brother, **who** lives in Shanghai, is a teacher.
2. The book, **which** cost me 100 yuan, is very good.
3. I gave him a book, **which** he liked very much.
4. My father, **whose** name is John, is a doctor.

#### which 代替整个主句
1. He didn't come, **which** surprised everyone.
2. She was ill, **which** is why she didn't come.
3. They missed the train, **which** made them late.

#### as 引导
1. **As is known to all**, China is a big country.
2. He is a good student, **as we all know**.
3. **As was reported**, the meeting was successful.

### 注意事项

1. **限制性 vs. 非限制性**：
   - 有限制性：无需逗号，翻译为"……的"
   - 非限制性：需要逗号，翻译为并列句

2. **非限制性定语从句不能用 that**：
   - 错误：My brother, that lives in Shanghai...
   - 正确：My brother, who lives in Shanghai...

3. **which 可以代替整个主句**：
   - He failed the exam, which made his parents sad.
   - This is the result, which we didn't expect.

4. **as vs. which**：
   - as 引导的从句可放句首或句中
   - which 引导的从句通常放句末

### 练习题

#### 一、选择填空

1. The man ____ helped me is my teacher.
   A. who B. which C. as D. what

2. My brother, ____ lives in Shanghai, is a teacher.
   A. who B. which C. that D. what

3. The book, ____ cost me 100 yuan, is very good.
   A. who B. which C. that D. what

4. He didn't come, ____ surprised everyone.
   A. who B. which C. that D. what

5. ____ is known to all, China is a big country.
   A. Who B. Which C. As D. What

#### 二、用适当的关系词填空

1. The book ____ I bought is interesting. （限制性）
2. My sister, ____ lives in Beijing, is a doctor. （非限制性）
3. He failed the exam, ____ made his parents sad. （非限制性）
4. The reason ____ he was late is unclear. （限制性）
5. ____ is known to all, the earth goes around the sun. （非限制性）

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. The book is very interesting. I bought it yesterday. （改为限制性定语从句）
   _________________________________________________
2. My teacher is kind. He teaches English. （改为非限制性定语从句）
   _________________________________________________
3. He was late. That made everyone angry. （用 which 合并）
   _________________________________________________
4. China is a big country. As we all know. （合并为一句）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. A（限制性，who 指人）
2. A（非限制性，who 指人，不能用 that）
3. B（非限制性，which 指物）
4. B（which 代替整个主句）
5. C（as 引导非限制性定语从句）

**二、**
1. which/that    2. who    3. which    4. why    5. As

**三、**
1. The book (which/that) I bought yesterday is very interesting.
2. My teacher, who teaches English, is kind.
3. He was late, which made everyone angry.
4. As is known to all, China is a big country.
