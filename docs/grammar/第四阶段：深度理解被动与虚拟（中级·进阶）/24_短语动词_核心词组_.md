# 短语动词（核心词组）

## 常用词组/搭配

look after, give up, turn on, take care of

## 详细笔记

### 一、短语动词概述

#### 1. 定义
- 短语动词是由动词 + 介词或动词 + 副词构成的固定搭配
- 相当于一个整体，表达特定含义

#### 2. 类型

| 类型 | 结构 | 例词 |
|------|------|------|
| 动词 + 介词 | 动词 + 介词（宾语放介词后） | look at, listen to, wait for |
| 动词 + 副词 | 动词 + 副词（宾语可放中间或后） | turn on, give up, put off |
| 动词 + 副词 + 介词 | 动词 + 副词 + 介词 | look forward to, catch up with |

### 二、核心动词+介词搭配

#### 1. look
- look at 看
- look after 照顾、保管
- look for 寻找
- look like 看起来像
- look up 查阅、向上看
- look down on 轻视
- look forward to 期待

#### 2. listen
- listen to 听

#### 3. wait
- wait for 等待
- wait on 服侍

#### 4. ask
- ask for 请求、要求
- ask sb. for sth. 向某人要某物

#### 5. speak/talk
- speak to/with 与……说话
- talk about 谈论
- talk to/with 与……谈话

#### 6. agree
- agree with sb. 同意某人
- agree to 同意（计划/建议）

### 三、核心动词+副词搭配

#### 1. turn
- turn on 打开
- turn off 关闭
- turn up 开大、调高
- turn down 关小、调低
- turn around 转身
- turn out 结果是

#### 2. put
- put on 穿上、戴上
- put off 推迟
- put away 放好、储存
- put up 张贴、搭建
- put down 放下、写下

#### 3. get
- get up 起床
- get on 上车
- get off 下车
- get along 相处、进展
- get back 返回
- get rid of 摆脱

#### 4. give
- give up 放弃
- give out 分发
- give away 赠送、泄露
- give in 屈服

#### 5. come
- come out 出来、出版
- come across 偶然遇见
- come back 回来
- come from 来自
- come true 实现

#### 6. go
- go out 外出、熄灭
- go over 复习、检查
- go through 通过、经历
- go away 走开
- go on 继续

### 四、动词+副词+介词

- look forward to 期待
- catch up with 赶上
- get along with 与……相处
- put up with 忍受
- do well in 在……做得好
- pay attention to 注意
- be made up of 由……组成

### 五、常考短语动词辨析

| 短语 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| look for vs. find | 寻找 vs. 找到 | I'm looking for my keys. I found them. |
| put on vs. wear | 穿上（动作） vs. 穿着（状态） | Put on your coat. She is wearing a red dress. |
| turn on vs. switch on | 打开（电器） | Turn on the light. |
| give up vs. give in | 放弃 vs. 屈服 | Don't give up. He gave in to the pressure. |
| look up vs. look up to | 查阅 vs. 尊敬 | Look up the word. We look up to our teachers. |

### 例句

#### 动词 + 介词
1. Please **look at** the blackboard.
2. I **look after** my younger sister every day.
3. She is **looking for** her lost keys.
4. He **listens to** music every evening.
5. We **wait for** the bus at the station.
6. Please **ask for** help when you need it.
7. I **agree with** you on this point.
8. They **talk about** the movie last night.

#### 动词 + 副词
1. Please **turn on** the light. / Please **turn** the light **on**.
2. It's cold. **Put on** your coat.
3. Don't **put off** the meeting.
4. **Give up** smoking. It's bad for your health.
5. The sun **goes out** at 6 every morning.
6. I **got up** early this morning.
7. **Get on** the bus carefully.
8. **Put down** your books.

#### 动词 + 副词 + 介词
1. I **look forward to** hearing from you.
2. We **catch up with** the others.
3. She **gets along well with** her classmates.
4. You must **put up with** the noise.
5. Please **pay attention to** your pronunciation.

#### 短语辨析
1. I'm **looking for** my pen. I **found** it just now.
2. **Put on** your coat. It's cold outside.
3. She is **wearing** a beautiful dress today.
4. Please **turn on** the TV. / Please **turn** the TV **on**.
5. Don't **give up**. Keep trying.
6. He **gave in** to his demands.

### 注意事项

1. **动词+副词词组中，宾语位置**：
   - 名词宾语可放中间或后：turn on the light = turn the light on
   - 代词宾语必须放中间：turn it on（正确）/ turn on it（错误）

2. **look forward to 后接动名词**：
   - 错误：I look forward to hear from you.
   - 正确：I look forward to hearing from you.

3. **give up 后接动名词**：
   - 错误：Give up to smoke.
   - 正确：Give up smoking. / Give up smoking cigarettes.

4. **put off 后接动名词**：
   - 错误：Put off to go.
   - 正确：Put off going. / Put off the meeting.

5. **不及物动词无被动语态**：
   - 错误：The accident was happened last night.
   - 正确：The accident happened last night.

6. **常见易混淆短语**：
   - look for（寻找）vs find（找到）
   - look up（查阅）vs look up to（尊敬）
   - put on（穿上）vs wear（穿着状态）

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的短语动词填空

1. Please ____ the blackboard. （看）
2. She ____ her younger brother every day. （照顾）
3. I am ____ my lost watch. （寻找）
4. Please ____ your coat. It's cold. （穿上）
5. Don't ____ the work. Finish it today. （推迟）
6. He ____ smoking last year. （放弃）
7. We are going to ____ the exam. （复习）
8. I ____ my pen just now. （找到）
9. Please ____ the light. It's dark. （打开）
10. She ____ well ____ her classmates. （相处）

   A. hearing B. hear C. to hear D. to hearing

4. Please turn ____ the TV. I want to watch it.
   A. on B. off C. up D. down

5. He got ____ the bus and went home.
   A. on B. off C. up D. down
