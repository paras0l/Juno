# 非谓语动词——动名词

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 26 |
| 语法点 | 非谓语动词——动名词 |
| 核心内容 | doing作主语、宾语 |

## 常用词组/搭配

look forward to, be used to, can't help, feel like

## 详细笔记

### 一、动名词概述

#### 1. 定义
- 动名词是动词的另一种非谓语形式，由动词原形 + -ing 构成
- 兼具动词和名词的特征

#### 2. 形式
- **肯定**：doing
- **否定**：not doing
- **被动**：being done

### 二、动名词的作用

#### 1. 作主语
- **Swimming** is good for your health.
- **Reading** books is my hobby.
- 注意：it 作形式主语：It is no use **complaining**.

#### 2. 作表语
- My hobby is **reading**.
- His job is **teaching** English.

#### 3. 作宾语
- I enjoy **reading** books.
- She is good at **swimming**.

#### 4. 作定语
- a **sleeping** bag = a bag for sleeping
- a **reading** room = a room for reading
- 注意：动名词作定语是名词性质，不是动词性质

### 三、必须接动名词的动词

| 动词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| enjoy | 喜欢 | I enjoy **reading**. |
| finish | 完成 | I have finished **reading** the book. |
| practice | 练习 | She practices **playing** the piano. |
| mind | 介意 | Would you mind **opening** the window? |
| suggest | 建议 | I suggest **going** there. |
| keep | 继续 | Keep **working**. |
| include | 包括 | The plan includes **finishing** the work. |
| consider | 考虑 | I consider **moving** to another city. |

### 四、常用动名词短语

| 短语 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| look forward to | 期待 | I look forward to **hearing** from you. |
| be used to | 习惯于 | I am used to **getting** up early. |
| can't help | 忍不住 | I can't help **laughing**. |
| feel like | 想要 | I feel like **eating** ice cream. |
| be worth | 值得 | The book is worth **reading**. |
| be tired of | 厌倦 | I am tired of **doing** this. |
| spend...in | 花费（时间） | I spend two hours **in reading** every day. |
| have trouble/difficulty in | 在……有困难 | I have trouble **in solving** the problem. |

### 五、动名词和不定式的区别

#### 1. 意义区别
- I like **reading**. （习惯性动作）
- I like **to read** this book. （特定动作）

#### 2. 常见动词对比

| 动词 | 接动名词 | 接不定式 |
|------|----------|----------|
| like/love | doing（习惯） | to do（具体） |
| hate | doing/to do | doing/to do |
| prefer | doing/to do | doing/to do |
| remember | doing（已做） | to do（未做） |
| forget | doing（已做） | to do（未做） |
| stop | doing（停下不做了） | to do（停下去做） |
| go on | doing（继续做同一事） | to do（改做另一事） |
| try | doing（尝试做） | to do（努力做） |
| need | doing（需要被做） | to do（需要去做） |

#### 3. 表语用法
- My job is **teaching**. （动名词作表语 = 名词）
- My job is **to teach**. （不定式作表语 = 具体任务）

### 例句

#### 作主语
1. **Reading** is good for your mind.
2. **Swimming** is my favorite sport.
3. **Taking** a walk after dinner is healthy.

#### 作表语
1. My hobby is **reading**.
2. His job is **teaching** English.
3. The important thing is **learning** hard.

#### 作宾语
1. I enjoy **reading** books.
2. She finished **writing** the letter.
3. He practices **playing** the piano every day.

#### 作定语
1. a **sleeping** bag
2. a **reading** room
3. a **swimming** pool

#### 常用短语
1. I **look forward to meeting** you soon.
2. I **am used to getting** up early.
3. I **can't help laughing** when I see this.
4. I **feel like having** a rest.
5. This book is **worth reading**.
6. I **spend** two hours **reading** every day.
7. I have trouble **in solving** this problem.
8. Would you mind **opening** the window?

#### 动名词 vs. 不定式
1. I like **reading**. （习惯）/ I like **to read** this book. （这次）
2. I remember **meeting** him before. （记得做过）
3. Remember **to meet** him tomorrow. （记得要做）
4. Stop **talking**. （别说了）/ Stop **to talk**. （停下来去说话）

### 注意事项

1. **动名词作主语时，谓语用单数**：
   - Reading books **is** my hobby.
   - Swimming **is** good for health.

2. **动名词作定语和现在分词作定语的区别**：
   - 动名词作定语（名词性质）：a reading room（阅读室）/ a sleeping bag（睡袋）
   - 现在分词作定语（动词性质）：a running boy（正在跑的男孩）/ a sleeping baby（正在睡觉的婴儿）

3. **动名词的复合结构**：
   - His **coming** late made us angry.
   - It's no use **your crying**.

4. **look forward to 后必须接动名词**：
   - 错误：I look forward to meet you.
   - 正确：I look forward to meeting you.

5. **need/require/want + doing = need/require/want to be done**：
   - The car needs **washing**. = The car needs **to be washed**.

6. **remember/forget/stop/try 等动词后动名词和不定式的区别**：
   - remember doing：记得做过（已发生）
   - remember to do：记得要做（未发生）
   - stop doing：停止做（不做这个了）
   - stop to do：停下来去做（开始做新的）

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给动词的适当形式填空

1. I enjoy ____ (read) books.
2. She finished ____ (write) the letter.
3. I look forward to ____ (meet) you soon.
4. He is used to ____ (get) up early.
5. I can't help ____ (laugh) when I hear this.
6. This book is worth ____ (read).
7. I spent two hours ____ (read) yesterday.
8. Would you mind ____ (open) the window?

#### 二、选择题

1. I like ____ in the morning.
   A. running B. to run C. run D. ran

2. Remember ____ the letter before you go out.
   A. posting B. to post C. posted D. post

3. Stop ____ ! It's time for class.
   A. talk B. talking C. to talk D. talked

4. I am tired of ____ the same thing every day.
   A. do B. doing C. done D. to do

5. The car needs ____.
   A. wash B. washing C. washed D. to washing

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. Reading is my hobby. （用 it 改写）
   _________________________________________________
2. He enjoys reading. He also enjoys swimming. （合并）
   _________________________________________________
3. I want to see a movie. （用 feel like 改写）
   _________________________________________________
4. It's important to learn English. （用 no use 改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. reading    2. writing    3. meeting    4. getting    5. laughing    6. reading    7. reading    8. opening

**二、**
1. A（like doing 习惯）
2. B（remember to do 记得要做）
3. B（stop doing 停止做）
4. B（be tired of doing）
5. B（need doing = need to be done）

**三、**
1. It is my hobby reading.
2. He enjoys reading and swimming.
3. I feel like seeing a movie.
4. It's no use learning English. / Learning English is no use.
