## 常用词组/搭配

as if, wish, would rather, It's time that...

## 详细笔记

### 一、虚拟语气概述

#### 1. 定义
- 虚拟语气用于表达与事实相反的假设、愿望、建议等
- 不描述真实情况，而是假设、想象或不可能的情况

#### 2. 虚拟语气的三种情况

| 类型 | 时间 | 从句结构 | 主句结构 |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| 与现在事实相反 | 现在 | 过去式（be 用 were） | would/should/could/might + 动词原形 |
| 与过去事实相反 | 过去 | had + 过去分词 | would/should/could/might have + 过去分词 |
| 与将来事实相反 | 将来 | should + 动词原形 / were to + 动词原形 | would/should/could/might + 动词原形 |

### 二、if 条件句的虚拟语气

#### 1. 与现在事实相反
- **结构**：If + 主语 + 过去式（be 用 were）, 主语 + would + 动词原形
- **例**：If I **were** you, I **would go** there. （但我不是你）
- **例**：If I **had** money, I **would buy** a car. （但我没有钱）

#### 2. 与过去事实相反
- **结构**：If + 主语 + had + 过去分词, 主语 + would have + 过去分词
- **例**：If I **had studied** hard, I **would have passed** the exam. （但我没学习）
- **例**：If he **had come** yesterday, he **would have seen** her. （但他昨天没来）

#### 3. 与将来事实相反
- **结构**：If + 主语 + should/were to + 动词原形, 主句结构同上
- **例**：If it **should rain** tomorrow, I would stay at home. （不太可能下雨）
- **例**：If I **were to** be rich, I would travel around the world.

### 三、wish 后的虚拟语气

#### 1. 与现在事实相反
- **结构**：Wish + 主语 + 过去式（be 用 were）
- **例**：I wish I **were** a bird. （但我不是）
- **例**：I wish I **knew** the answer. （但我不知道）

#### 2. 与过去事实相反
- **结构**：Wish + 主语 + had + 过去分词
- **例**：I wish I **had studied** harder. （但我之前没努力）
- **例**：I wish I **had met** her before. （但我没见过）

#### 3. 与将来事实相反
- **结构**：Wish + 主语 + would/could + 动词原形
- **例**：I wish it **would stop** raining. （但雨停不了）

### 四、其他虚拟语气结构

#### 1. as if / as though（好像）
- 结构：从句用虚拟语气
- 与现在相反：as if he **knew** the truth
- 与过去相反：as if he **had seen** a ghost

#### 2. would rather（宁愿）
- **结构**：would rather + 过去式（与现在相反）/ had + 过去分词（与过去相反）
- 例：I would rather I **were** younger. （但我不能）
- 例：I would rather he **had gone** there yesterday. （但他没去）

#### 3. It's time that...（是……时候了）
- **结构**：It's time that + 主语 + 过去式
- **例**：It's time that we **left**. = It's time for us to leave.

#### 4. suggestion/demand/recommend 等后的虚拟语气
- **结构**：suggest (that) 主语 (should) + 动词原形
- **例**：I suggest that he **should go** there.
- **类似动词**：demand, require, recommend, propose, insist, request 等

### 五、虚拟语气的倒装

#### 1. 省略 if 的倒装
- If I were you, I would go. → **Were** I you, I would go.
- If he had come, he would have seen. → **Had** he come, he would have seen.
- If it should rain, I would stay. → **Should** it rain, I would stay.

#### 2. 倒装的使用条件
- 条件：从句含有 were, had, should 时，可以省略 if 并倒装

### 例句

#### 与现在事实相反
1. If I **were** you, I **would take** the job.
2. If he **knew** the truth, he **would be** angry.
3. If I **had** more money, I **would buy** a bigger house.

#### 与过去事实相反
1. If I **had studied** harder, I **would have passed** the exam.
2. If she **had come** yesterday, she **would have met** him.
3. If we **had left** earlier, we **wouldn't have missed** the train.

#### 与将来事实相反
1. If it **should rain** tomorrow, I would stay home.
2. If I **were to** win the lottery, I would travel around the world.

#### wish 句型
1. I wish I **were** a bird. （与现在相反）
2. I wish I **had studied** harder. （与过去相反）
3. I wish it **would stop** raining. （与将来相反）

#### as if / as though
1. He talks **as if** he **knew** everything. （与现在相反）
2. She looks **as though** she **had seen** a ghost. （与过去相反）

#### would rather
1. I would rather I **were** younger. （与现在相反）
2. I would rather he **had come** yesterday. （与过去相反）

#### It's time that
1. It's time that we **went** home.
2. It's time that you **finished** your homework.

### 注意事项

1. **if 条件句中 be 动词的虚拟形式**：
   - 无论主语是什么，be 动词用 were
   - 错误：If I was you...
   - 正确：If I were you...

2. **if 条件句的倒装**：
   - 省略 if，将 were/had/should 提到句首
   - If I were you, I would go. → Were I you, I would go.
   - If he had come, he would have seen. → Had he come, he would have seen.

3. **wish 后虚拟语气的时间判断**：
   - wish + 过去式 → 与现在事实相反
   - wish + had + 过去分词 → 与过去事实相反
   - wish + would/could + 动词 → 与将来事实相反

4. **suggest 等动词后的虚拟语气**：
   - suggest that 主语 (should) + 动词原形
   - should 可以省略
   - 例：I suggest that he (should) go there.

5. **It's time 后的虚拟语气**：
   - 用过去式，不用 would/should
   - 正确：It's time that we left.
   - 错误：It's time that we should leave.

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给动词的适当形式填空

1. If I ____ (be) you, I would help him.
2. If he ____ (know) the truth, he would be angry.
3. If I ____ (have) enough money, I would buy a car.
4. If she ____ (come) yesterday, she would have met him.
5. If I ____ (study) harder, I would have passed the exam.
6. I wish I ____ (be) a bird.
7. I wish I ____ (meet) her before.
8. It's time that we ____ (go) home.

#### 二、选择题

1. If I ____ you, I would take the job.
   A. am B. was C. were D. be

2. If he ____ harder, he would have passed.
   A. studied B. had studied C. study D. studies

3. I wish I ____ the answer.
   A. know B. knew C. had known D. will know

4. It's time that we ____ home.
   A. go B. went C. going D. to go

5. He talks as if he ____ everything.
   A. knows B. knew C. had known D. knowing

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. If I were you, I would take the job. （用 wish 改写）
   _________________________________________________
2. He didn't come. He didn't see her. （用 if 虚拟语气合并）
   _________________________________________________
3. If it rains tomorrow, I will stay home. （用虚拟语气改写）
   _________________________________________________
4. Were I you, I would go there. （还原为 if 句）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. were    2. knew    3. had    4. had come    5. had studied    6. were    7. had met    8. went

**二、**
1. C（与现在事实相反，be 用 were）
2. B（与过去事实相反，had + 过去分词）
3. B（wish + 过去式，与现在相反）
4. B（It's time that + 过去式）
5. B（as if + 过去式，与现在相反）

**三、**
1. I wish I were you. I would take the job.
2. If he had come yesterday, he would have seen her.
3. If it should rain tomorrow, I would stay home. / If it were to rain tomorrow, I would stay home.
4. If I were you, I would go there.
