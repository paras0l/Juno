# 非谓语动词——不定式

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 25 |

| 核心内容 | to do作主语、宾语、定语、状语 |

## 常用词组/搭配

too...to, enough to, in order to, be said to

## 详细笔记

### 一、不定式概述

#### 1. 定义
- 不定式是动词的一种非谓语形式，由 to + 动词原形构成
- 在句子中不能单独作谓语，但可以充当多种成分

#### 2. 形式
- **肯定**：to do
- **否定**：not to do
- **被动**：to be done
- **进行**：to be doing

### 二、不定式的作用

#### 1. 作主语
- **To learn** English is important. = It is important **to learn** English.
- **To be** honest is not easy.

#### 2. 作表语
- My dream is **to be** a doctor.
- His job is **to teach** English.

#### 3. 作宾语
- I want **to see** a movie.
- She likes **to read** books.
- **注意**：有些动词后只能接不定式：want, hope, decide, plan, agree, refuse, manage 等

#### 4. 作宾语补足语
- I ask him **to help** me.
- She told me **to come** early.
- **注意**：有些动词后不带 to：see, hear, watch, feel, let, make, have 等

#### 5. 作定语
- I have a book **to read**.
- I have nothing **to say**.
- The first student **to arrive** will get a prize.

#### 6. 作状语
- 表目的：I came here **to see** you.
- 表原因：I am happy **to meet** you.
- 表结果：He is too tired **to walk**.

### 三、不定式的特殊句型

#### 1. too...to（太……而不能……）
- He is **too tired to walk**.
- = He is so tired that he cannot walk.

#### 2. enough to（足够……可以……）
- He is **old enough to go** to school.
- = He is so old that he can go to school.

#### 3. be said/believed/reported to（据说/据报道）
- He **is said to be** a great scientist.
- The book **is reported to be** interesting.

#### 4. It takes sb. time to do（做某事花费某人时间）
- It **takes** me **two hours to finish** the work.

#### 5. It is + adj. + for/of sb. to do（对某人来说……）
- It is **important for** you **to study** hard.
- It is **kind of** you **to help** me.
- **区别**：for 表示客观，of 表示主观评价

### 四、不带 to 的不定式

#### 1. 感官动词后
- see, hear, watch, feel, notice + sb. + do（全过程）/ doing（正在）
- I saw him **cross** the street.
- I saw him **crossing** the street.

#### 2. 使役动词后
- let, make, have + sb. + do
- Let him **go**. / Make her **laugh**.

#### 3. 情态动词后
- can/should/must + do
- You should **finish** the work.

#### 4. why not + do
- Why not **go** there together?

### 五、不定式和动名词的区别

| 动词 | 接不定式 | 接动名词 |
|------|----------|----------|
| want/plan/agree/decide/refuse | to do | — |
| like/love/enjoy | to do / doing | doing |
| remember/forget | to do（未做）/ doing（已做） |
| stop | to do（停下去做）/ doing（停下不做了） |
| go on | to do（继续做）/ doing（继续做同一事） |

### 例句

#### 作主语
1. **To learn** a language takes time.
2. **To be** a good teacher is not easy.
3. It is important **to study** hard.

#### 作表语
1. My dream is **to be** a doctor.
2. His job is **to teach** students.

#### 作宾语
1. I want **to see** a movie.
2. She hopes **to pass** the exam.
3. He decides **to leave** tomorrow.

#### 作宾补
1. I ask him **to help** me.
2. She told me **to wait** for her.
3. We expect you **to come** on time.

#### 作定语
1. I have a book **to read**.
2. There is nothing **to worry** about.
3. He is the first **to finish**.

#### 作状语
1. I came here **to see** you. （目的）
2. I am happy **to meet** you. （原因）
3. He is too tired **to walk**. （结果）

#### 特殊句型
1. He is **too young to understand**. （太……而不能）
2. She is **old enough to go** to school. （足够……可以）
3. It **takes** me two hours **to finish** the work. （花费时间）
4. It is **important for** you **to study** hard. （对……来说）
5. He **is said to be** a genius. （据说）

#### 不带 to 的不定式
1. I saw him **cross** the street. （全过程）
2. Let him **go**. （使役动词）
3. Why not **have** a rest? （why not）

### 注意事项

1. **不定式作主语常置于句末，用 it 作形式主语**：
   - 错误：To learn English is important for me.
   - 正确：To learn English is important. / It is important to learn English.

2. **感官动词和使役动词后的不定式 to 要省略**：
   - I made him **laugh**. （正确）
   - I made him **to laugh**. （错误）
   - I saw him **leave**. （正确）
   - I saw him **to leave**. （错误）

3. **不定式的被动形式**：
   - The work **to be done** is important.
   - He is the person **to be punished**.

4. **too...to 和 enough to 的否定**：
   - He is too tired to walk. = He is not energetic enough to walk.
   - 这种结构本身就表示"太……而不能"。

5. **不定式可以有自己的逻辑主语**：
   - For him to finish this work is difficult.
   - It is difficult for him to finish this work.

6. **常见接不定式的动词**：want, hope, decide, plan, agree, refuse, ask, expect, pretend, manage 等。

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给动词的适当形式填空

1. I want ____ (see) a movie.
2. It's important ____ (study) hard.
3. I ask him ____ (help) me.
4. He is too young ____ (go) to school.
5. She is old enough ____ (take) care of herself.
6. It takes me two hours ____ (finish) the work.
7. I saw him ____ (cross) the street.
8. Let him ____ (go) first.

#### 二、选择题

1. It is important ____ English every day.
   A. learning B. to learn C. learn D. learned

2. He is the first ____ the exam.
   A. pass B. passed C. to pass D. passing

3. She is too tired ____.
   A. to walk B. walking C. walk D. walks

4. I saw him ____ the room.
   A. enter B. to enter C. entering D. entered

5. It's kind of you ____ me.
   A. to help B. help C. helping D. helped

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. To learn English is important. （用 it 改写）
   _________________________________________________
2. He is very old. He can go to school. （用 enough to 改写）
   _________________________________________________
3. I want to see a movie. （改为否定句）
   _________________________________________________
4. He is too tired. He can't walk. （用 too...to 改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. to see    2. to study    3. to help    4. to go    5. to take    6. to finish    7. cross    8. go

**二、**
1. B（it is important to do）
2. C（the first to do）
3. A（too...to）
4. A（see sb. do）
5. A（it is kind of sb. to do）

**三、**
1. It is important to learn English.
2. He is old enough to go to school.
3. I don't want to see a movie.
4. He is too tired to walk.
