## 常用词组/搭配

generally speaking, compared with

## 详细笔记

### 一、分词概述

#### 1. 定义
- 分词是动词的另一种非谓语形式，分为现在分词和过去分词
- 现在分词：动词原形 + -ing（表示主动和进行）
- 过去分词：动词过去式（表示被动和完成）

#### 2. 形式
- **现在分词**：doing / being done / having done
- **过去分词**：done / having been done

### 二、分词的作用

#### 1. 作定语
- **位置**：名词前或名词后
- **现在分词**：The **sleeping** baby is cute. / The boy **running** is my brother.
- **过去分词**：The **broken** window needs repair. / The book **written** by him is popular.

#### 2. 作状语
- 表示时间、原因、条件、结果、方式等
- **现在分词**：表主动，正在进行
- **过去分词**：表被动，已完成
- **例**：**Seeing** the movie, I felt happy.（时间）
- **例**：**Given** more time, I can finish it.（条件）

#### 3. 作表语
- The story is **interesting**. （现在分词作表语，主动含义）
- The man is **interested**. （过去分词作表语，被动含义）

#### 4. 作宾语补足语
- I saw him **reading** a book. （正在读书）
- I heard the song **sung** by her. （被唱的歌）

### 三、现在分词和过去分词的区别

| 特征 | 现在分词 | 过去分词 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 意义 | 主动、进行 | 被动、完成 |
| 用法 | 修饰主动执行动作的事物 | 修饰承受动作的事物 |
| 例句 | a **developing** country（发展中国家） | a **developed** country（发达国家） |
| 例句 | an **exciting** movie（令人兴奋的电影） | an **excited** audience（兴奋的观众） |

### 四、常见分词短语

| 短语 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| generally speaking | 一般来说 | Generally speaking, the weather is good. |
| compared with | 与……相比 | Compared with you, I am still young. |
| judging from | 从……判断 | Judging from his accent, he is from Shanghai. |
| considering | 考虑到 | Considering his age, he did well. |
| given | 考虑到、给定 | Given the time, we have to hurry. |
| supposing | 假设 | Supposing it rains, what will we do? |

### 五、分词与连词的转换

#### 1. 现在分词 = 时间/原因/条件连词 + 主语 + 动词
- **When** I saw him, I said hello. → **Seeing** him, I said hello.
- **Because** he was tired, he went to bed. → **Being** tired, he went to bed.

#### 2. 过去分词 = 连词 + 主语 + be 动词 + 过去分词
- **If** it is done, it will be good. → **Done**, it will be good.
- **When** it is finished, please call me. → **Finished**, please call me.

### 六、分词的否定形式

- **结构**：not + 分词
- **例**：Not **knowing** the answer, I kept silent.
- **例**：Not **finished** the work, I couldn't go home.

### 例句

#### 作定语
1. The **sleeping** baby is so cute. （现在分词，表主动进行）
2. The **broken** window needs to be fixed. （过去分词，表被动完成）
3. The girl **standing** there is my sister. （现在分词后置）
4. The book **written** by him is very popular.

#### 作状语
1. **Seeing** the movie, I felt very happy. （时间）
2. **Given** more time, I can finish the work. （条件）
3. **Walking** in the park, I met my old friend. （时间）
4. **Because** he was tired, he went to bed. → **Being** tired, he went to bed. （原因）

#### 作表语
1. The story is very **interesting**. （令人感兴趣的）
2. The audience is very **excited**. （感到兴奋的）
3. The news is **surprising**. （令人惊讶的）
4. He looks **tired**. （看起来疲惫的）

#### 作宾补
1. I saw him **reading** a book. （正在读）
2. I heard her **singing** in the room. （正在唱）
3. I found the window **broken**. （被发现破了）
4. He kept the money **locked** in the box.

#### 分词短语
1. **Generally speaking**, the weather is nice in spring.
2. **Compared with** you, I am still young.
3. **Judging from** his accent, he must be from Guangdong.
4. **Considering** his age, he did very well.

### 注意事项

1. **现在分词和过去分词作定语的区别**：
   - 现在分词：主动、进行（a sleeping baby = 正在睡觉的宝宝）
   - 过去分词：被动、完成（a broken window = 被打破的窗户）

2. **interesting vs. interested**：
   - interesting：令人感兴趣的（事物本身特性）
   - interested：感兴趣的（人的感受）
   - 类似：exciting/excited, surprising/surprised, relaxing/relaxed

3. **分词作状语时，主语必须一致**：
   - 错误：Walking in the park, the flowers are beautiful.
   - 正确：Walking in the park, I saw beautiful flowers.

4. **with + 宾语 + 分词（独立结构）**：
   - With the work finished, we went home.
   - He fell asleep with the light on.

5. **分词的完成式**：having done（先于主句动作发生）
   - Having finished the work, he went home.

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给动词的适当形式填空

1. The ____ (sleep) baby is so cute.
2. The ____ (break) window needs to be fixed.
3. ____ (see) the movie, I felt happy.
4. ____ (give) more time, I can finish it.
5. The story is very ____ (interest).
6. He looks ____ (tire).
7. I saw him ____ (run) in the park.
8. ____ (general) speaking, it's a good plan.

#### 二、选择题

1. The ____ students are very excited.
   A. interesting B. excited C. exciting D. bore

2. I heard her ____ in the next room.
   A. sing B. singing C. to sing D. sang

3. ____ the weather, we will go out.
   A. Considering B. Considered C. Consider D. To consider

4. The movie is very ____.
   A. interesting B. interested C. interest D. interestingly

5. With the work ____, we went home.
   A. finish B. finished C. finishing D. to finish

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. Because he was tired, he went to bed. （用分词改写）
   _________________________________________________
2. The book is written by him is popular. （用分词改写）
   _________________________________________________
3. He finished the work. He went home. （用分词合并）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. sleeping    2. broken    3. Seeing    4. Given    5. interesting    6. tired    7. running    8. Generally

**二、**
1. B（excited 感到兴奋的）
2. B（hear sb. doing）
3. A（considering 考虑到）
4. A（interesting 令人感兴趣的）
5. B（with + 宾语 + 过去分词）

**三、**
1. Being tired, he went to bed. / Tired, he went to bed.
2. Written by him, the book is popular.
3. Having finished the work, he went home.
