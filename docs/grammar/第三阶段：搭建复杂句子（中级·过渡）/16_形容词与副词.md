# 形容词与副词

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 16 |
| 语法点 | 形容词与副词 |
| 核心内容 | 比较级/最高级、enough/too用法 |

## 常用词组/搭配

as...as, more and more, the more...the more

## 详细笔记

### 一、形容词

#### 1. 形容词的位置

| 位置 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 名词前 | 限定词 + 形容词 + 名词 | a **beautiful** girl |
| 系动词后 | 主语 + be + 形容词 | The girl is **beautiful** |
| 宾语补足语 | 动词 + 宾语 + 形容词 | keep the room **clean** |
| 表语 | 主语 + 系动词 + 形容词 | She looks **happy** |

#### 2. 形容词的排序（记忆口诀：美小圆旧黄法国木书房）

- 美(beautiful)小(small)圆(round)旧(old)黄(yellow)法(French)国(friendly)木(wooden)书(interesting)房(fashionable)
- 例：a beautiful small round old yellow French wooden interesting fashionable room

#### 3. 形容词的比较级和最高级

##### 规则变化

| 规则 | 原级 | 比较级 | 最高级 |
|------|------|--------|--------|
| 一般加 -er/est | tall | taller | tallest |
| 以 e 结尾只加 -r/st | nice | nicer | nicest |
| 辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i + er/est | happy | happier | happiest |
| 重读闭音节结尾，双写 + er/est | big | bigger | biggest |
| 多音节词前加 more/most | beautiful | more beautiful | most beautiful |

##### 不规则变化

| 原级 | 比较级 | 最高级 |
|------|--------|--------|
| good/well | better | best |
| bad/badly | worse | worst |
| many/much | more | most |
| little | less | least |
| far | farther/further | farthest/furthest |
| old | older/elder | oldest/eldest |

### 二、副词

#### 1. 副词的分类

| 分类 | 例词 |
|------|------|
| 方式副词 | quickly, slowly, carefully |
| 时间副词 | now, today, yesterday, already |
| 频率副词 | always, often, sometimes, never |
| 程度副词 | very, quite, too, enough |
| 地点副词 | here, there, everywhere |

#### 2. 副词的比较级和最高级

- 规则与形容词相同（多音节词用 more/most）
- 例：quickly → more quickly → most quickly

### 三、比较级句型

#### 1. 同级比较：as...as

- **肯定**：主语 + as + 形容词/副词 + as + 比较对象
- 例：He is **as tall as** his brother.
- **否定**：主语 + not as/so + 形容词/副词 + as + 比较对象
- 例：He is **not as/so tall as** his brother.

#### 2. 差级比较：比较级 + than

- 主语 + 形容词/副词比较级 + than + 比较对象
- 例：He is **taller than** his brother.
- 注意事项：比较对象要对称，避免重复

#### 3. 最高级：the + 最高级 + in/of

- He is **the tallest in** our class.
- She is **the most beautiful of** all the girls.

### 四、enough 和 too 的用法

#### 1. enough

- **结构**：形容词/副词 + enough + to do sth.
- **例**：He is **old enough to go** to school.
- **名词前**：enough + 名词
- **例**：I have **enough money** to buy it.

#### 2. too

- **结构**：too + 形容词/副词 + to do sth.
- **例**：He is **too tired to walk**.
- **注意**：too...to 表结果时，主语和动词有逻辑关系

#### 3. enough vs. too

| 句型 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| adj./adv. + enough to do | He is tall enough to reach it. |
| too + adj./adv. + to do | He is too young to go to school. |
| not too...to do | He is not too old to learn. |
| too...not to do | He is too angry not to say it. |

### 五、倍数表达

- 主语 + 倍数 + as + 形容词/副词 + as
  - This room is **three times as big as** that one.
- 主语 + 倍数 + 比较级 + than
  - This room is **twice bigger than** that one.
- 主语 + 倍数 + the size/length/height of
  - This room is **twice the size of** that one.

### 例句

#### 形容词位置
1. She is a **beautiful** girl. （名词前）
2. The book is **interesting**. （系动词后）
3. We keep the room **clean**. （宾补）
4. He looks **happy**. （表语）

#### 比较级
1. This book is **more interesting than** that one.
2. He is **taller than** me.
3. She is **the most beautiful** in our class.
4. This is **the best** movie I have ever seen.

#### 同级比较
1. He runs **as fast as** his brother.
2. She is **not as/so tall as** her sister.
3. He works **as hard as** anyone else.

#### enough/too
1. He is **old enough to go** to school.
2. The book is **interesting enough to read** again.
3. He is **too tired to walk**.
4. The problem is **too difficult to solve**.

#### 倍数
1. This room is **three times as big as** that one.
2. She is **twice older than** her brother.
3. This river is **three times the length of** that one.

### 注意事项

1. **比较级前可用 much/a lot/even/still 修饰**：
   - much taller, even better, a lot more beautiful

2. **比较级避免重复**：
   - 错误：He is taller than he is taller than me.
   - 正确：He is taller than me. / He is taller than I am.

3. **the more...the more 句型**（越……越……）：
   - The **more** you practice, the **better** you become.
   - 注意：比较级前不加 the（the more 中的 the 是定冠词）

4. **比较级和最高级前不加 very**：
   - 错误：very taller, very best
   - 正确：much taller, far better

5. **as...as 中间用形容词/副词原级**：
   - 错误：He is as more careful as his brother.
   - 正确：He is as careful as his brother.

6. **older/eldest 和 older/eldest 的区别**：
   - older/oldest：年龄上更老/最老
   - elder/eldest：排行上的年长/最长
   - my elder brother（我的哥哥）/ an old friend（老朋友）

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给词的适当形式填空

1. This book is ____ (interesting) than that one.
2. He is ____ (tall) in our class.
3. She runs ____ (fast) than her brother.
4. This is ____ (good) movie I have ever seen.
5. He is ____ (old) enough to go to school.
6. The problem is too ____ (difficult) to solve.

#### 二、选择题

1. He is ____ than his brother.
   A. tall B. taller C. tallest D. the tallest

2. She is the ____ student in our class.
   A. most hard-working B. more hard-working C. hardest-working D. harder-working

3. This room is ____ that one.
   A. three times big as B. three times as big as C. three times bigger D. three times bigger as

4. He is ____ to understand this.
   A. enough smart B. smart enough C. too smart D. very smart

5. The ____ you study, the ____ you will get.
   A. harder, better B. more hard, more good C. harder, gooder D. more harder, better

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. He is taller than his brother. （用 not as...as 改写）
   _________________________________________________
2. This book is interesting. That book is more interesting. （合并为一句）
   _________________________________________________
3. He is old. He can go to school. （用 enough 改写）
   _________________________________________________
4. She is very beautiful. （用比较级改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. more interesting    2. the tallest    3. faster    4. the best    5. old enough    6. difficult

**二、**
1. B（than 提示用比较级）
2. A（最高级加 most）
3. B（倍数 + as + 形容词 + as）
4. B（enough + to do）
5. A（the more..., the more...）

**三、**
1. He is not as tall as his brother. / He is not so tall as his brother.
2. That book is more interesting than this one. / This book is not as interesting as that one.
3. He is old enough to go to school.
4. She is more beautiful than before. / She is even more beautiful.
