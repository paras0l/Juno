# 时间状语从句

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 19 |
| 语法点 | 时间状语从句 |
| 核心内容 | when/while/as/before/after/until |

## 常用词组/搭配

as soon as, by the time, every time

## 详细笔记

### 一、时间状语从句定义

- **定义**：用从属连词引导，表示主句动作发生时间的从句
- **结构**：主句 + 从属连词 + 从句 / 从属连词 + 从句 + 主句
- **特点**：主句和从句的时态需要呼应

### 二、常见时间状语连词

#### 1. when（当……时）

- **用法**：强调时间点或时间段
- **主句时态**：可与进行时或一般时态连用
- **例**：I was reading **when** he came in.

#### 2. while（当……时）

- **用法**：强调两个动作同时进行（持续性动词）
- **例**：I was reading **while** she was cooking.

#### 3. as（当……时）

- **用法**：表示"一边……一边……"或时间较短的从句
- **例**：He sang **as** he walked.

#### 4. before（……之前）

- **例**：I finished my homework **before** my mother came home.

#### 5. after（……之后）

- **例**：I went home **after** I finished my work.

#### 6. until/till（直到……）

- **例**：Wait here **until** I come back. / I didn't leave **until** he came.

#### 7. since（自从……）

- **例**：I have lived here **since** 2015.

#### 8. as soon as（一……就……）

- **例**：I will call you **as soon as** I arrive.

#### 9. once（一……就……）

- **例**：**Once** you understand this, you'll pass the exam.

#### 10. every time/each time（每次）

- **例**：**Every time** I see her, she smiles at me.

### 三、时态呼应规则

| 主句 | 从句 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般将来时 | 一般现在时 | if/when/after 等表将来 |
| 主句过去时 | 从句过去时 | 两个动作都发生在过去 |
| 主句过去完成时 | 从句过去时 | 从句先于主句 |
| 主句现在时 | since 从句过去时 | since 引导的时间起点 |

### 四、难点解析

#### 1. when vs. while vs. as

| 连词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| when | 时间点（动作可长可短） | When I arrived, she was cooking. |
| while | 持续性动作，同时进行 | While I was reading, she was cooking. |
| as | 伴随，或时间较短 | As I was walking, it started to rain. |

#### 2. not...until（直到……才……）

- **结构**：主句 + not + until + 时间
- **例**：I **didn't understand** until he explained it. （直到他解释我才明白）
- **倒装**：Not until he explained it did I understand.

#### 3. by the time（到……时）

- **用法**：主句用完成时
- **例**：**By the time** I got there, the train **had left**.

### 例句

#### when
1. I was reading a book **when** he came in.
2. **When** I grow up, I want to be a doctor.
3. **When** she arrived, the meeting started.

#### while
1. I was reading **while** she was cooking.
2. **While** you were sleeping, I finished my work.
3. He called me **while** I was having dinner.

#### as
1. He sang **as** he walked.
2. **As** the sun set, we went home.
3. **As** I was leaving, I saw her.

#### before/after
1. I finished my homework **before** dinner.
2. **After** I finished my work, I went to bed.
3. She had left **before** I arrived.

#### until
1. Wait here **until** I come back.
2. I didn't know the truth **until** she told me.
3. **Not until** he explained did I understand.

#### as soon as
1. I will call you **as soon as** I get home.
2. **As soon as** she saw him, she smiled.
3. Please let me know **as soon as** you arrive.

#### since
1. I have lived here **since** 2015.
2. **Since** she left, I have been very sad.
3. It is three years **since** I last saw her.

### 注意事项

1. **时间状语从句中用一般现在时代替一般将来时**：
   - 错误：When he will come, I will tell you.
   - 正确：When he comes, I will tell you.

2. **until vs. not...until**：
   - until：直到（主句动词是持续性）
   - not...until：直到……才（主句动词是短暂性）
   - I waited until 6 o'clock. / I didn't leave until 6 o'clock.

3. **while 后不能接非进行时动词**：while 后通常接进行时或持续性动作。

4. **since 后的时间点**：since + 具体时间点/过去式从句，主句用完成时。

5. **as soon as 的位置**：可放句首或句中，放句首时不用逗号隔开。

6. **主句和从句的时间先后**：
   - before/after：主句和从句有先后顺序
   - when/while/as：两个动作同时发生

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的连词填空

1. ____ I was reading, he came in.
2. I will call you ____ I get home.
3. He had left ____ I arrived.
4. I didn't understand ____ he explained.
5. She has lived here ____ 2010.
6. ____ you study hard, you will pass.
7. I was cooking ____ my mother came back.
8. ____ the time I arrived, everyone had left.

#### 二、选择题

1. I was reading a book ____ the light went out.
   A. when B. while C. as D. before

2. ____ she arrived, the meeting started.
   A. When B. While C. As D. Before

3. Please call me ____ you reach the airport.
   A. as soon as B. before C. after D. until

4. He has worked here ____ five years ago.
   A. since B. for C. in D. on

5. I ____ understand until he explained.
   A. don't B. didn't C. not D. no

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. When/While/As    2. as soon as / when    3. before / when    4. until / when    5. since    6. If / When    7. when / while    8. By the time

**二、**
1. A（when + 时间点，light went out 是短暂动词）
2. A（when 引导时间状语）
3. A（as soon as 一……就……）
4. A（since + 具体时间点，用完成时）
5. B（not...until 结构）

**三、**
1. As soon as I get home, I will call you. / I will call you as soon as I get home.
2. When he came in, I was reading a book. / I was reading a book when he came in.
3. I left after he came. / I didn't leave until he came.
