# 原因状语从句

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 20 |
| 语法点 | 原因状语从句 |
| 核心内容 | because/since/as/for |

## 常用词组/搭配

now that, seeing that

## 详细笔记

### 一、原因状语从句定义

- **定义**：用从属连词引导，说明原因或理由的从句
- **结构**：主句 + 从属连词 + 从句 / 从属连词 + 从句 + 主句

### 二、常见原因状语连词

#### 1. because（因为）

- **用法**：语气最强，回答"why"问句
- **位置**：可放句首或句中
- **例**：I like English **because** it's interesting.
- **注意**：because 不与 so 同时使用

#### 2. since（由于、既然）

- **用法**：语气较弱，表示已知事实
- **位置**：常放句首
- **例**：**Since** you are here, let's start.

#### 3. as（因为）

- **用法**：语气较弱，说明原因
- **位置**：常放句首
- **例**：**As** it is raining, we stay at home.

#### 4. for（因为）

- **用法**：表示原因（多用于书面语）
- **位置**：不能放句首，放在两个句子之间
- **例**：I stayed at home, **for** I was ill.

#### 5. now that（既然）

- **例**：**Now that** you are here, you can help me.

#### 6. seeing that（由于、鉴于）

- **例**：**Seeing that** everyone is here, let's start.

### 三、because vs. since vs. as vs. for

| 连词 | 语气 | 位置 | 回答 why | 与 so 连用 |
|------|------|------|-----------|------------|
| because | 最强 | 句中/句首 | 可以 | 不能 |
| since | 较弱 | 句首 | 不常 | 不能 |
| as | 较弱 | 句首/句中 | 不常 | 不能 |
| for | 最弱 | 句中 | 不能 | 不能 |

### 四、原因状语从句的省略

#### 1. 省略主语
- 因为……，所以……（主语相同时可省略）
- 例：As he was tired, (he) went to bed early.

#### 2. 省略 be 动词
- 例：While (she was) reading, she fell asleep.

### 五、Because of vs. Because

| 类型 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| because of + 名词/短语 | 介词短语 | I didn't go out **because of** the rain. |
| because + 从句 | 从句 | I didn't go out **because** it was raining. |

### 六、常用句型

#### 1. The reason why...is that...
- **例**：The reason **why** I left early **is that** I was tired.

#### 2. That's because...
- **例**：I was late. That's **because** the traffic was bad.

#### 3. That's why...
- **例**：I studied hard. That's **why** I passed the exam.

### 例句

#### because
1. I study English **because** I like it.
2. **Because** he was ill, he didn't come to school.
3. She is happy **because** she passed the exam.

#### since
1. **Since** you are busy, I'll ask someone else.
2. **Since** it's already late, we should go home.
3. **Since** you agree, let's start.

#### as
1. **As** she was tired, she went to bed early.
2. I didn't go out **as** it was raining.
3. **As** he is my best friend, I trust him.

#### for
1. I stayed at home, **for** I was feeling ill.
2. The ground is wet, **for** it rained last night.
3. He must be hungry, **for** he didn't have breakfast.

#### now that
1. **Now that** everyone is here, let's begin.
2. **Now that** you are older, you should be more careful.

#### 常用句型
1. The reason **why** I came late **is that** I missed the bus.
2. I failed the exam. That's **because** I didn't study hard.
3. I studied hard. That's **why** I passed the exam.

### 注意事项

1. **because 不与 so 连用**：
   - 错误：Because it rained, so we stayed at home.
   - 正确：Because it rained, we stayed at home. / It rained, so we stayed at home.

2. **because 后接从句，because of 后接名词/短语**：
   - 错误：Because of it rained, we stayed at home.
   - 正确：Because it rained, we stayed at home. / Because of the rain, we stayed at home.

3. **since 和 as 表示原因时**，主句不能是否定句（因为是已知事实）。

4. **for 引导的原因状语不能放句首**：
   - 错误：For I was ill, I stayed at home.
   - 正确：I stayed at home, for I was ill.

5. **The reason why...is that... 结构**：
   - The reason why I am late is that I missed the bus.
   - that 不能用 because 替代。

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的连词填空

1. I stayed at home ____ it was raining.
2. ____ you are here, let's start.
3. He failed the exam ____ he didn't study.
4. I am late, ____ the traffic was bad.
5. ____ now you understand, you should work harder.
6. ____ everyone is ready, we can begin.
7. I didn't go to school ____ I was sick.
8. She is happy, ____ she got a good job.

#### 二、选择题

1. He was absent ____ he was ill.
   A. because B. for C. since D. as

2. ____ it's getting late, we should leave.
   A. Since B. Because C. For D. Because of

3. The reason ____ I came late is ____ I missed the bus.
   A. why, because B. why, that C. that, because D. for, that

4. I didn't go out ____ the rain.
   A. because B. because of C. for D. since

5. I am happy ____ you are here.
   A. because B. for C. since D. as

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. It was raining. We stayed at home. （用 because 合并）
   _________________________________________________
2. He was ill. He didn't come to school. （用 because of 改写）
   _________________________________________________
3. I didn't study. I failed the exam. （用 that's why 改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. because / as / for    2. Since / As    3. because    4. for / because    5. Now that    6. Since / Now that    7. because / as    8. because / for

**二、**
1. A（because 回答 why）
2. A（since 表示已知事实）
3. B（reason why...is that... 结构）
4. B（because of + 名词）
5. A（because 表示原因）

**三、**
1. Because it was raining, we stayed at home. / We stayed at home because it was raining.
2. He didn't come to school because of his illness.
3. I didn't study, that's why I failed the exam.
