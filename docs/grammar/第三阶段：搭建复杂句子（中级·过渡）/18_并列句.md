# 并列句

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 18 |
| 语法点 | 并列句 |
| 核心内容 | and/but/or/so/for连接两个独立分句 |

## 常用词组/搭配

not only...but also, either...or, neither...nor

## 详细笔记

### 一、并列句定义

- **定义**：用并列连词连接两个或两个以上意义相关、地位平等的句子
- **结构**：句子1 + 并列连词 + 句子2

### 二、常见并列连词

#### 1. and（和、而且）

- **用法**：连接意义相近的句子
- **例**：I like singing **and** she likes dancing.
- **注意**：and 连接两个动词时，主语相同时可省略第二个主语

#### 2. but（但是）

- **用法**：连接意义相反的句子
- **例**：I am tired **but** I want to continue.

#### 3. or（或者、否则）

- **用法**：表示选择或条件
- **例**：Do you want tea **or** coffee? / Hurry up, **or** you'll be late.

#### 4. so（所以）

- **用法**：表示结果
- **例**：It was late, **so** I went home.

#### 5. for（因为）

- **用法**：表示原因（多用于书面语）
- **例**：I stayed at home, **for** I was ill.

### 三、复合并列连词

#### 1. not only...but also（不仅……而且……）

- **结构**：Not only + 句子1, **but also** + 句子2
- **注意**：not only 放句首时，主句倒装
- **例**：**Not only** is he a teacher, **but also** a writer.

#### 2. either...or（或者……或者……）

- **结构**：Either + 句子1, **or** + 句子2
- **注意**：就近原则
- **例**：**Either** you come to my house, **or** I'll go to yours.

#### 3. neither...nor（既不……也不……）

- **结构**：Neither + 句子1, **nor** + 句子2
- **注意**：两者都否定，就近原则
- **例**：**Neither** is he happy, **nor** am I.

#### 4. not...but（不是……而是……）

- **结构**：Not + 句子1, **but** + 句子2
- **例**：**Not** because I don't like it, **but** because I have no time.

### 四、并列句与复合句的区别

| 类型 | 定义 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| 并列句 | 两个独立句子用并列连词连接 | I came and she left. |
| 复合句 | 主句 + 从句（从属连词引导） | When I came, she left. |
| 并列复合句 | 并列句中包含从句 | I came and when she saw me, she left. |

### 五、并列句中的省略

#### 1. 相同主语省略
- I like English **and** (I) study it every day.

#### 2. 相同谓语省略
- He can sing **and** (can) dance.

#### 3. 相同成分省略
- I have finished my work **and** (so has) she.

### 例句

#### 基本并列连词
1. I like singing **and** she likes dancing.
2. He is young **but** he works very hard.
3. Do you want tea **or** coffee?
4. It was late, **so** I went home.
5. I stayed at home, **for** I was ill.

#### 复合并列连词
1. **Not only** does he speak English, **but also** French.
2. **Either** you come with me, **or** I'll go alone.
3. **Neither** is he a teacher, **nor** am I.
4. **Not** because I don't like it, **but** because I have no time.

#### 省略
1. I came **and** (I) saw her.
2. He can sing **and** (can) dance.
3. I finished the work **and** (so did) she.

#### 就近原则
1. **Either** you **or** he is wrong.
2. **Neither** he **nor** I am wrong.

### 注意事项

1. **并列连词不能连接从句**：并列连词只连接平等的两个句子，不能引导从句。

2. **so 和 for 的位置**：
   - so 表示结果，通常位于第二句开头
   - for 表示原因，多用于书面语，位于第二句中间

3. **not only...but also 的主谓一致**：
   - 遵循就近原则：Not only he but also I am right.

4. **either...or / neither...nor 的主谓一致**：
   - 两者都遵循就近原则
   - Either you or I am wrong.

5. **祈使句 + or 表示警告**：
   - Hurry up, or you'll be late.
   - Don't be late, or you'll miss the train.

6. **避免中式英语**：
   - 错误：Because he is rich, so he is happy.
   - 正确：Because he is rich, he is happy. / He is rich, so he is happy.

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的并列连词填空

1. I like English ____ Math.
2. He was tired, ____ he still kept working.
3. Are you a student ____ a teacher?
4. Work hard, ____ you'll fail.
5. ____ only is he clever, ____ also kind.

#### 二、选择题

1. I like coffee ____ she likes tea.
   A. and B. but C. or D. so

2. ____ you ____ me is wrong.
   A. Either, nor B. Neither, or C. Either, or D. Neither, nor

3. He didn't come to the meeting, ____ he was busy.
   A. and B. but C. for D. so

4. Not only she but also I ____ happy.
   A. am B. is C. are D. be

5. Hurry up, ____ you'll be late.
   A. and B. but C. or D. so

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. He is a teacher. He is also a writer. （用 not only...but also 改写）
   _________________________________________________
2. You can go there. You can stay here. （用 either...or 改写）
   _________________________________________________
3. He is not happy. I am not happy. （用 neither...nor 改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. and / but    2. but / so    3. or    4. or    5. Not only, but also

**二、**
1. A（and 连接两个并列句子）
2. D（neither...nor 两者都否定）
3. C（for 表示原因）
4. A（就近原则，I 用 am）
5. C（or 表示否则）

**三、**
1. He is not only a teacher but also a writer.
2. You can either go there or stay here.
3. Neither he nor I am happy.
