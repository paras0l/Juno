# 情态动词

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 15 |
| 语法点 | 情态动词 |
| 核心内容 | can/could, may/might, must, should, would |

## 常用词组/搭配

be able to, have to, ought to

## 详细笔记

### 一、情态动词概述

- **定义**：情态动词本身有词义，但没有人称和数的变化，不能单独作谓语
- **特点**：后接动词原形，无变化
- **位置**：情态动词 + 动词原形

### 二、主要情态动词

#### 1. can / could

| 用法 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 能力 | 能，会 | I **can** speak English. |
| 许可 | 可以 | You **can** use my pen. |
| 可能 | 可能 | It **can** rain later. |
| 请求 | 能……吗 | **Can** you help me? |
| could | 过去能力/礼貌 | He **could** swim when he was 5. |

#### 2. may / might

| 用法 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 许可 | 可以 | You **may** sit here. |
| 可能 | 可能 | She **may** come today. |
| 祝愿 | 祝…… | **May** you be happy! |
| might | 较礼貌/不确定 | She **might** be at home. |

#### 3. must

| 用法 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 义务 | 必须 | You **must** finish the work. |
| 禁止 | 禁止 | You **must not** smoke here. |
| 肯定推测 | 一定 | He **must** be at home. |

#### 4. should

| 用法 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 建议 | 应该 | You **should** study harder. |
| 义务 | 应该 | You **should** help others. |
| 可能 | 可能 | He **should** arrive soon. |

#### 5. would

| 用法 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 过去将来 | 将要 | I thought he **would** come. |
| 意愿 | 想要 | I **would** like some water. |
| 请求 | 愿……吗 | **Would** you like some tea? |
| 习惯 | 过去常常 | He **would** read books every day. |

### 三、常见情态动词用法对比

#### can vs. may
- can：侧重能力，may：侧重许可
- You can swim.（你会游泳）/ You may swim.（你可以游泳）

#### must vs. have to
- must：主观认为必须
- have to：客观要求必须
- I must finish this.（我认为必须完成）/ I have to go to school.（不得不去上学）

#### must vs. can (表推测)
- must：肯定推测（一定）
- can：否定推测（不可能）
- He must be at home.（他一定在家）/ He can't be at home.（他不可能在家）

#### may be vs. maybe
- may be：可能是（动词短语）
- maybe：也许（副词）
- He **may be** right. / **Maybe** he is right.

### 四、情态动词的否定形式

| 情态动词 | 否定形式 | 含义 |
|----------|----------|------|
| can | cannot / can't | 不能 |
| could | couldn't | 不能/不会 |
| may | may not | 可能不 |
| must | must not / mustn't | 禁止/不应该 |
| should | should not / shouldn't | 不应该 |
| would | wouldn't | 不会/不愿 |

### 五、情态动词后接的动词形式

| 形式 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 动词原形 | 常规用法 | You **must come** here. |
| have + 过去分词 | 表示应该已经 | You **should have finished**. |
| be + 现在分词 | 表示可能正在 | She **may be waiting** for you. |
| be + 过去分词 | 表示被动 | The work **must be done**. |

### 例句

#### can/could
1. I **can** speak three languages.
2. **Could** you help me with my homework?
3. She **could** read when she was 4.
4. You **can't** smoke here.

#### may/might
1. You **may** use my computer.
2. She **might** be at home.
3. **May** I come in?
4. It **might** rain later.

#### must
1. You **must** finish your homework first.
2. You **mustn't** tell anyone.
3. He **must** be very tired.

#### should
1. You **should** study harder.
2. He **should** apologize to her.
3. **Should** I tell him the truth?

#### would
1. **Would** you like some tea?
2. I **would** like to go with you.
3. He **would** always help others.

#### 情态动词表推测
1. He **must be** in the office.
2. She **may be** busy.
3. They **can't be** students.
4. He **should be** home by now.

### 注意事项

1. **情态动词后接动词原形**：情态动词不能独立作谓语，后接动词原形。
   - 错误：He can to swim.
   - 正确：He can swim.

2. **mustn't 的含义**：不是"不必"，而是"禁止、不允许"。
   - You mustn't smoke here. = 禁止吸烟

3. **can 和 could 的区别**：
   - can：现在的能力/许可
   - could：过去的能力/更礼貌的请求

4. **must 和 have to 的区别**：
   - must：主观意愿
   - have to：客观需要
   - You must finish this. vs. I have to go to school.

5. **must 没有过去式**：表示过去必须，用 had to。
   - I had to finish the work yesterday.

6. **情态动词表推测的力度**：
   - must > should > may > might > could
   - must 否定式用 can't（不可能）

7. **would like to do = want to do**：would like 比 want 更礼貌。

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的情态动词填空

1. You ____ (必须) finish your homework first.
2. ____ I use your phone? （我可以……吗）
3. She ____ (可能) be at home now.
4. You ____ (不应该) smoke here.
5. ____ you like some coffee? （你想要……吗）
6. He ____ (能) speak English and French.
7. You ____ (必须不能/禁止) tell anyone.
8. We ____ (应该) help each other.

   A. must B. can C. may D. should

4. You ____ smoke in the hospital.
   A. can B. may C. must D. mustn't

5. ____ you like some tea?
   A. Do B. Can C. Would D. Should
