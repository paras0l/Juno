# 现在进行时

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 09 |
| 语法点 | 现在进行时 |
| 核心内容 | 此刻正在发生 |

## 常用词组/搭配

now, at the moment, Look!/Listen!

## 详细笔记

### 一、定义与作用

- **定义**：表示说话时正在发生或进行的动作
- **作用**：强调动作正在此刻或当前阶段进行

### 二、构成

#### 句型：主语 + be (am/is/are) + 动词-ing

| 主语 | be 动词 | 例句 |
|------|---------|------|
| I | am | I **am reading** a book. |
| you | are | You **are watching** TV. |
| he/she/it | is | He **is sleeping**. She **is cooking**. |
| we/they | are | We **are playing** games. |

### 三、动词-ing 形式变化规则

| 规则 | 例词 |
|------|------|
| 一般加 -ing | working, reading, playing |
| 以不发音 e 结尾，去 e 加 -ing | make → making, write → writing, dance → dancing |
| 重读闭音节结尾，双写最后一个辅音 + -ing | run → running, swim → swimming, sit → sitting |
| 以 ie 结尾，变 ie 为 y + -ing | die → dying, tie → tying, lie → lying |

### 四、时间标志词

| 标志词 | 用法 |
|--------|------|
| now | 现在 |
| at the moment | 此刻 |
| right now | 此刻 |
| Look! / Listen! | 常用于引起注意 |
| these days | 这几天 |
| at present | 目前 |

### 五、现在进行时的特殊用法

#### 1. 表暂时的情况
- He **is staying** with his friends these days.（他这几天住在朋友家）

#### 2. 表反复发生的动作（与 always, constantly 等连用）
- She **is always complaining**.（她总是抱怨）
- They **are constantly moving** furniture.（他们不停地搬家具）

#### 3. 表将来（位置移动的动词）
- come, go, leave, start, arrive, fly 等可用进行时表将来
- I **am coming** to see you tomorrow.
- The train **is leaving** soon.

### 例句

#### 基本句型
1. I **am reading** a book now.
2. You **are watching** TV at the moment.
3. He **is playing** basketball.
4. We **are studying** English.

#### 正在进行
1. Look! The children **are playing** in the park.
2. Listen! Someone **is singing** in the next room.
3. I **am doing** my homework right now.
4. She **is cooking** dinner in the kitchen.

#### 暂时的情况
1. He **is staying** at his friend's house this week.
2. She **is working** as a tutor during the holiday.
3. They **are living** in Beijing these days.

#### 表将来
1. I **am going** to the supermarket later.
2. She **is leaving** for Shanghai tomorrow.
3. The train **is arriving** in 10 minutes.

#### 与 always 连用（表反复）
1. He **is always making** noise.
2. She **is constantly complaining** about her job.

### 注意事项

1. **be 动词不能省略**：进行时必须有 be 动词。
   - 错误：He playing basketball.
   - 正确：He is playing basketball.

2. **静态动词不能用进行时**：表示状态、感官的动词通常不用进行时。
   - 错误：I am knowing the answer.
   - 正确：I know the answer.
   - 常见静态动词：know, believe, see, hear, like, love, want, have（表示拥有）

3. **双写规则要记牢**：必须是重读闭音节结尾且结尾是单一辅音。
   - run → running ✓
   - open → opening ✓（以元音结尾，不双写）

4. **有些动词意义不同**：根据语境判断是否用进行时。
   - She is thinking.（正在思考）vs. She thinks.（她认为）
   - He is seeing a doctor.（正在看医生）vs. He sees a doctor.（他通常看医生）

5. **表将来的动词**：只有位置移动动词能用现在进行时表将来。
   - come, go, leave, arrive, fly, start, drive 等

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给动词的适当形式填空

1. Look! The boy ____ (run) in the park.
2. I ____ (read) a book now.
3. She ____ (cook) dinner at the moment.
4. They ____ (play) games in the classroom.
5. He ____ (not, sleep). He ____ (watch) TV.
6. ____ you ____ (listen) to music?
7. The teacher ____ (talk) with students.
8. We ____ (have) English class now.

#### 二、选择题

1. Listen! Someone ____ at the door.
   A. knocks B. is knocking C. knock D. knocked

2. I can't talk now. I ____ my homework.
   A. do B. does C. am doing D. doing

3. He always ____ others, it's very impolite.
   A. is criticizing B. criticizes C. criticizing D. Criticize

4. The train ____ soon. We should hurry.
   A. leaves B. is leaving C. leave D. left

5. What ____ you ____ these days?
   A. do, do B. are, doing C. did, do D. is, doing

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. He is playing football. （改为否定句）
   _________________________________________________
2. She is studying English. （改为一般疑问句）
   _________________________________________________
3. They are watching TV. （用 now 改写句子）
   _________________________________________________
4. He is always late for school. （用 today 改写句子）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. is running    2. am reading    3. is cooking    4. are playing    5. is not sleeping, is watching    6. Are, listening    7. is talking    8. are having

**二、**
1. B（正在敲门，用现在进行时）
2. C（正在做作业，用现在进行时）
3. A（always + 进行时，表反复）
4. B（用现在进行时表将来）
5. B（these days 提示用现在进行时）

**三、**
1. He is not playing football.
2. Is she studying English?
3. They are watching TV now.
4. He is late for school today.
