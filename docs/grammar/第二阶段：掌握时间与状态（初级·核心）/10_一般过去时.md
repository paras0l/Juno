# 一般过去时

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 10 |
| 语法点 | 一般过去时 |
| 核心内容 | 过去的事实/动作 |

## 常用词组/搭配

yesterday, last week, ...ago, just now

## 详细笔记

### 一、定义与作用

- **定义**：表示过去某个时间发生的动作或存在的状态
- **作用**：描述过去的动作、事件、状态

### 二、构成

#### 1. 行为动词的一般过去时

- **肯定句**：主语 + 动词过去式 + ...
- **否定句**：主语 + did not (didn't) + 动词原形 + ...
- **疑问句**：Did + 主语 + 动词原形 + ...?

#### 2. be 动词的一般过去时

- **肯定句**：主语 + was/were + ...
- **否定句**：主语 + was/were + not + ...
- **疑问句**：Was/Were + 主语 + ...?

| 主语 | be 动词过去式 |
|------|---------------|
| I | was |
| he/she/it | was |
| we/you/they | were |

### 三、动词过去式变化规则

#### 规则变化

| 规则 | 例词 |
|------|------|
| 一般加 -ed | worked, played, wanted |
| 以 e 结尾只加 -d | liked, lived, hoped |
| 辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i + -ed | studied, carried, tried |
| 重读闭音节结尾，最后一个辅音双写 + -ed | stopped, planned, dropped |

### 四、时间状语

| 词/短语 | 例句 |
|---------|------|
| yesterday | I went to school yesterday. |
| last week/month/year | She was busy last week. |
| ... ago | He left ten minutes ago. |

| 表达 | 中文 |

### 例句

#### 行为动词过去式
1. I **watched** a movie yesterday.
2. She **studied** English last night.
3. They **played** football after school.
4. He **ate** breakfast at 7:00 this morning.
5. We **went** to the park yesterday.

#### be 动词过去式
1. I **was** at home yesterday.
2. He **was** busy last week.
3. They **were** happy last year.
4. She **was** a student two years ago.
5. We **were** in the library just now.

#### 否定句
1. I **didn't go** to school yesterday.
2. She **wasn't** at home last night.
3. They **weren't** happy about the news.
4. He **didn't eat** breakfast this morning.

#### 疑问句
1. **Did** you **see** the movie yesterday?
2. **Was** she at home last night?
3. **Where did** you **go** just now?
4. **What did** he **do** yesterday?

#### 时间状语使用
1. I visited my grandparents **last weekend**.
2. She studied hard **two days ago**.
3. They moved to this city **in 2019**.
4. He was very busy **just now**.

### 注意事项

1. **be 动词过去式的选择**：单数主语用 was，复数主语用 were。
   - I/He/She/It was...
   - We/You/They were...

2. **否定句的构成**：不管行为动词还是 be 动词，否定句都加 not。
   - 行为动词：did + not + 动词原形
   - be 动词：was/were + not

3. **疑问句的构成**：did 提前，动词还原为原形。
   - 错误：Did he went to school?
   - 正确：Did he go to school?

4. **不规则动词需要单独记忆**：常见的 100 多个不规则动词是基础。

5. **ago 和 before 的区别**：
   - ago：从现在往前推，置于时间词后
   - before：从过去往前推，可单独使用
   - 例：three days ago / before three days

6. **last 和 last 的区别**：
   - last + 时间：上一个……（last Monday 上周一）
   - the last + 时间：最后一个……（the last one 最后一个）

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给动词的适当形式填空

1. I ____ (go) to school yesterday.
2. She ____ (be) at home last night.
3. They ____ (play) games after school.
4. He ____ (have) breakfast at 7:00 this morning.
5. We ____ (see) a movie yesterday.
6. I ____ (not, eat) anything this morning.
7. She ____ (be) a student two years ago.
8. They ____ (not, come) to the party yesterday.

#### 二、选择题

1. He ____ English last year.
   A. study B. studied C. studies D. studying

2. ____ you at home yesterday?
   A. Were B. Was C. Are D. Is

3. She ____ to the supermarket yesterday.
   A. go B. goes C. went D. going

4. I ____ anything yesterday.
   A. don't do B. didn't do C. doesn't do D. not do

5. They ____ happy last week.
   A. is B. are C. were D. was

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. He went to school yesterday. （改为否定句）
   _________________________________________________
2. She was busy last night. （改为一般疑问句）
   _________________________________________________
3. They played games after school. （对画线部分提问）
   _________________________________________________
4. I had breakfast at 7:00. （用 yesterday 改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. went    2. was    3. played    4. had    5. saw    6. didn't eat    7. was    8. didn't come

**二、**
1. B（last year 用一般过去时）
2. A（you 复数，用 were）
3. C（yesterday 用一般过去时）
4. B（yesterday 过去时间，用 didn't do）
5. C（last week 过去时间，复数用 were）

**三、**
1. He didn't go to school yesterday.
2. Was she busy last night?
3. What did they do after school?
4. I had breakfast yesterday at 7:00. / Yesterday I had breakfast at 7:00.
