# 过去进行时

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 11 |
| 语法点 | 过去进行时 |
| 核心内容 | 过去某个时刻正在发生 |

## 常用词组/搭配

at that time, while, when

## 详细笔记

### 一、定义与作用

- **定义**：表示过去某一时刻正在进行的动作
- **作用**：描述过去某个时间正在发生或进行的动作

### 二、构成

#### 句型：主语 + was/were + 动词-ing

| 主语 | be 动词过去式 | 例句 |
|------|--------------|------|
| I | was | I **was reading** at 7:00 last night. |
| he/she/it | was | He **was sleeping** when I came in. |
| we/you/they | were | They **were playing** games at that time. |

### 三、时间标志词

| 标志词 | 用法 |
|--------|------|
| at that time | 在那时 |
| at 7:00 last night | 在昨晚7点 |
| this time yesterday | 昨天这时候 |
| when + 过去时间 | 当……时候 |
| while + 进行时 | 当……时候（同时） |
| all day yesterday | 昨天一整天 |
| from...to... | 从……到…… |

### 四、when 和 while 的用法

#### 1. when + 短暂动词（过去进行时 + when + 过去时）
- 动作 A 发生时，动作 B 突然发生
- 例：I **was walking** in the park **when** I met her.

#### 2. while + 持续动词（过去进行时 + while + 过去进行时）
- 两个动作同时进行
- 例：I **was reading** while she **was cooking**.

#### 3. 关键区别
| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| when | was/were doing + when + did | I was sleeping when he came in. |
| while | while + was/were doing | While I was sleeping, he came in. |

### 五、过去进行时的用法

#### 1. 描述过去某个时刻正在进行的动作
- I **was studying** at 9:00 last night.
- They **were playing** basketball at that time.

#### 2. 描述故事发生的背景
- The sun **was shining** and the birds **were singing**.
- I **was walking** in the forest. Suddenly, I heard a strange sound.

#### 3. 与短暂动词连用，表突然发生
- I **was reading** a book when the phone **rang**.
- She **was cooking** when the door **opened**.

### 例句

#### 表示过去某一时刻正在进行的动作
1. I **was doing** my homework at 8:00 last night.
2. She **was cooking** when I called her.
3. They **were playing** games at that time.
4. He **was sleeping** at this time yesterday.

#### 与 when/while 连用
1. I **was reading** a book **when** the light went out.
2. While she **was cooking**, the phone rang.
3. They **were watching** TV **while** I **was studying**.
4. When he **arrived**, we **were having** dinner.

#### 描述故事背景
1. The sun **was shining** and the wind **was blowing**.
2. I **was walking** in the park when I met my old friend.
3. She **was waiting** for the bus when it started to rain.

#### 时间状语
1. At that time, I **was living** in Beijing.
2. This time last year, we **were traveling** in Europe.
3. All day yesterday, they **were working** in the office.

### 注意事项

1. **过去进行时的构成**：was/were + 动词-ing，be 动词不能省略。
   - 错误：He playing basketball at that time.
   - 正确：He was playing basketball at that time.

2. **when 和 while 的使用要点**：
   - when 引导的句子中，主句用过去进行时，从句用一般过去时
   - while 引导的句子中，两个句子都可以用过去进行时
   - 例：I was reading when he came in. / While I was reading, he came in.

3. **短暂动词 vs 持续动词**：
   - 短暂动词（瞬间完成）：come, go, arrive, leave, knock, ring
   - 持续动词（持续进行）：read, write, sleep, eat, watch

4. **过去进行时 vs 一般过去时**：
   - 过去进行时：动作在某个时间正在发生
   - 一般过去时：动作已经完成
   - 例：I was reading a book.（正在读）vs. I read a book.（读完了）

5. **不能用于过去进行时的动词**：与现在进行时相同，状态动词、感官动词不用进行时。

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给动词的适当形式填空

1. I ____ (read) a book at 9:00 last night.
2. She ____ (cook) when I ____ (call) her.
3. They ____ (play) games at that time.
4. He ____ (sleep) when the phone ____ (ring).
5. We ____ (study) while they ____ (play).
6. While I ____ (walk) in the park, I met her.
7. At this time yesterday, I ____ (watch) TV.
8. She ____ (not, do) her homework when I came.

#### 二、选择题

1. I ____ TV when the light went out.
   A. watched B. was watching C. watch D. am watching

2. While she ____, the phone rang.
   A. cooks B. cooked C. is cooking D. was cooking

3. They were playing games ____ I was studying.
   A. when B. while C. after D. before

4. What ____ you ____ at this time yesterday?
   A. do, do B. did, do C. were, doing D. are, doing

5. When he arrived, we ____ dinner.
   A. had B. were having C. have D. are having

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. I was reading a book at that time. （改为否定句）
   _________________________________________________
2. They were playing games. （用 when the phone rang 改写）
   _________________________________________________
3. I was walking in the park. （用 this time yesterday 改写）
   _________________________________________________
4. While she was cooking, the phone rang. （用 when 改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. was reading    2. was cooking, called    3. were playing    4. was sleeping, rang    5. were studying, were playing    6. was walking    7. was watching    8. wasn't doing

**二、**
1. B（当灯熄灭时，我正在看电视，用过去进行时）
2. D（while 引导的时间状语从句，用过去进行时）
3. B（while 表同时）
4. C（at this time yesterday 用过去进行时）
5. B（when he arrived 是过去时间，主句用过去进行时）

**三、**
1. I was not reading a book at that time.
2. They were playing games when the phone rang.
3. I was walking in the park this time yesterday.
4. When the phone rang, she was cooking.
