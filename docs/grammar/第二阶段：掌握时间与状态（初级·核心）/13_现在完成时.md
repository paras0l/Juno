# 现在完成时

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 13 |
| 语法点 | 现在完成时 |
| 核心内容 | 过去发生影响现在/持续到现在 |

## 常用词组/搭配

already, yet, ever, never, since, for

## 详细笔记

### 一、定义与作用

- **定义**：表示过去发生或已经完成的动作，对现在有影响
- **作用**：描述过去的经验、成就，或从过去持续到现在的状态

### 二、构成

#### 句型：主语 + have/has + 过去分词

| 主语 | 助动词 |
|------|--------|
| I/you/we/they | have |
| he/she/it | has |

#### 肯定句：主语 + have/has + 过去分词
- I **have finished** my homework.
- She **has gone** to Beijing.

#### 否定句：主语 + have/has + not + 过去分词
- I **haven't finished** my homework.
- She **hasn't gone** to Beijing.

#### 疑问句：Have/Has + 主语 + 过去分词?
- **Have** you **finished** your homework?
- **Has** she **gone** to Beijing?

### 三、过去分词变化规则

#### 规则变化

| 规则 | 例词 |
|------|------|
| 一般加 -ed | worked, played |
| 以 e 结尾只加 -d | lived, liked |
| 辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i + -ed | studied, carried |
| 重读闭音节结尾，双写 + -ed | stopped, planned |

#### 不规则变化（需记忆）
| 原形 | 过去分词 |
|------|----------|
| go | gone |
| come | come |
| see | seen |
| eat | eaten |
| have/has | had |
| do | done |
| make | made |
| take | taken |
| write | written |
| read | read（发音 /ri:d/） |
| know | known |
| think | thought |
| find | found |
| tell | told |
| say | said |

### 四、时间标志词

| 标志词 | 用法 |
|--------|------|
| already | 已经（用于肯定句） |
| yet | 已经（用于否定句/疑问句） |
| ever | 曾经（用于疑问句） |
| never | 从未（用于否定句） |
| just | 刚刚 |
| before | 以前 |
| since | 自从（后接时间点） |
| for | 持续（后接时间段） |

#### since vs for
- **since + 时间点**：since 2010, since last week, since 8:00
- **for + 时间段**：for three years, for a long time, for two hours

### 五、现在完成时的用法

#### 1. 过去发生，现在有结果
- I **have finished** my work.（工作已完成）
- She **has lost** her keys.（她把钥匙弄丢了）

#### 2. 过去的经验
- **Have** you **ever been** to Japan?
- I **have never seen** such a beautiful sunset.

#### 3. 从过去持续到现在（状态/动作）
- I **have lived** here **for** 10 years.
- She **has studied** English **since** 2015.

### 例句

#### 过去发生，现在有结果
1. I **have finished** my homework.
2. She **has gone** to Beijing.
3. They **have bought** a new car.
4. He **has lost** his wallet.

#### 过去的经验
1. **Have** you **ever visited** the Great Wall?
2. I **have never seen** such a beautiful movie.
3. She **has already eaten** breakfast.
4. We **have just finished** the work.

#### 持续到现在
1. I **have lived** in Shanghai **for** 10 years.
2. She **has studied** English **since** 2015.
3. They **have been** friends **since** childhood.
4. He **has worked** here **for** three months.

#### 时间状语
1. I **have** already **finished** my homework. （already 用于肯定句）
2. **Have** you **finished** your homework **yet**? （yet 用于疑问句）
3. I **haven't** finished my homework **yet**. （yet 用于否定句）
4. **Have** you **ever** been to Japan? （ever 用于疑问句）
5. I **have never** been to Japan. （never 用于否定句）

### 注意事项

1. **现在完成时不能与明确过去时间连用**：
   - 错误：I have finished my homework yesterday.
   - 正确：I finished my homework yesterday.

2. **since 和 for 的使用**：
   - since + 时间点（从……开始）
   - for + 时间段（持续……时间）
   - 例：I have lived here since 2010. / I have lived here for 10 years.

3. **already/yet/ever/never 的位置**：
   - already/yet：放在动词和 have/has 之间，或句末
   - ever/never：放在动词和 have/has 之间

4. **have been to vs have gone to**：
   - have been to：去过（已回来）
   - have gone to：去了（未回来）
   - 例：He has been to Beijing.（去过北京，现在在这里）vs. He has gone to Beijing.（去北京了，不在这里）

5. **瞬间动词和持续动词**：
   - 瞬间动词（buy, lose, die, marry）不能与 for 连用表持续
   - 持续动词（live, work, study）可以与 for 连用
   - 可用：He has been dead for three years.
   - 不可用：He has died for three years.

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给动词的适当形式填空

1. I ____ (finish) my homework.
2. She ____ (go) to Beijing.
3. They ____ (already, finish) the work.
4. I ____ (never, be) to Japan.
5. He ____ (live) here for 10 years.
6. She ____ (study) English since 2015.
7. ____ you ____ (ever, meet) him before?
8. We ____ (not, finish) yet.

#### 二、选择题

1. I ____ my homework. Let's go out to play.
   A. finish B. finished C. have finished D. finishing

2. She ____ to Shanghai. She will be back next week.
   A. has gone B. has been C. went D. was

3. How long ____ you ____ here?
   A. have, lived B. did, live C. do, live D. are, living

4. I haven't seen him ____.
   A. yesterday B. last week C. before D. just now

5. He ____ never ____ a plane.
   A. has, ride B. have, ridden C. has, ridden D. had, ridden

**一、**
