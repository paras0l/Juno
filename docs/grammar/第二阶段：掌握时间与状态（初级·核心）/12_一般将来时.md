# 一般将来时

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 12 |
| 语法点 | 一般将来时 |
| 核心内容 | 将来发生的动作/计划 |

## 常用词组/搭配

tomorrow, next week, in the future

## 详细笔记

### 一、定义与作用

- **定义**：表示将来要发生的动作或计划
- **作用**：描述未来的计划、安排、预测

### 二、构成方式

#### 1. will + 动词原形

| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + will + 动词原形 | I **will go** to school tomorrow. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + will not (won't) + 动词原形 | I **won't go** to school tomorrow. |
| 疑问句 | Will + 主语 + 动词原形? | **Will** you **go** to school tomorrow? |

#### 2. be going to + 动词原形

| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + be going to + 动词原形 | I **am going to** study English. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + be not going to + 动词原形 | I **am not going to** study English. |
| 疑问句 | Be + 主语 + going to + 动词原形? | **Are** you **going to** study English? |

#### 3. be + 动词-ing（表将来）

- 用于位置移动的动词：come, go, leave, start, arrive, fly 等
- 例：I **am leaving** tomorrow. / The train **is arriving** soon.

### 三、will 和 be going to 的区别

| 特征 | will | be going to |
|------|------|--------------|
| **预测** | 基于当前情况推断 | 基于现在的证据 |
| **计划** | 临时决定 | 事先计划 |
| **意愿** | 表达意愿 | 表达意图 |
| **时间** | 用于将来任何时间 | 用于较近的将来 |

#### 用法对比
- I **will** help you.（临时决定）
- I **am going to** help you.（已经计划好）
- Look! It's going to rain.（根据天气预测）
- I think it will rain tomorrow.（主观预测）

### 四、时间状语

| 时间状语 | 用法 |
|----------|------|
| tomorrow | 明天 |
| tomorrow morning/afternoon/evening | 明天早上/下午/晚上 |
| next week/month/year | 下周/月/年 |
| soon | 很快 |
| in the future | 将来 |
| in + 时间段 | ……之后（in two days 两天后） |
| this afternoon/evening | 今天下午/晚上 |
| tonight | 今晚 |

### 五、其他表将来的方式

#### 1. be about to + 动词原形（即将）
- The train **is about to** leave.
- I **am about to** go out.

#### 2. be to + 动词原形（预定）
- The meeting **is to be** held next week.
- You **are to** finish the work today.

### 例句

#### will 用法
1. I **will** visit my grandparents tomorrow.
2. She **will** be a doctor in the future.
3. They **won't** come to the party tonight.
4. **Will** you help me with my homework?

#### be going to 用法
1. I **am going to** study abroad next year.
2. She **is going to** buy a new computer.
3. We **are going to** have a picnic this weekend.
4. **Are** you **going to** watch the movie?

#### be + 动词-ing 表将来
1. I **am leaving** for Beijing tomorrow.
2. The train **is arriving** in 10 minutes.
3. She **is coming** to see us soon.

#### will vs be going to
1. I think it **will** rain later.（预测）
2. Look at those clouds! It's **going to** rain.（根据证据预测）
3. I **will** help you with this bag.（临时决定）
4. I **am going to** have a birthday party this weekend.（计划）

#### 时间状语
1. We will go to Shanghai **next week**.
2. She will finish the work **tomorrow morning**.
3. They will have a meeting **in two days**.
4. I will call you **soon**.

### 注意事项

1. **will 和 be going to 的区别**：
   - will：表临时决定、预测、意愿
   - be going to：表事先计划、根据现有迹象预测

2. **be going to 的 be 动词不能省略**：
   - 错误：He going to study.
   - 正确：He is going to study.

3. **will 的否定缩写**是 won't，不是 won't。

4. **in + 时间段** 表示"……之后"，用于将来时：
   - in three days = 三天后
   - in a week = 一周后

5. **be going to 表将来的动词**：
   - 位置移动动词：come, go, leave, start, arrive, fly, drive
   - 其他：meet, see, do, have, play 等

6. **时间状语位置**：可放句首或句尾。
   - Tomorrow I will go to school. = I will go to school tomorrow.

### 练习题

#### 一、用 will 或 be going to 填空

1. I ____ (visit) my grandparents tomorrow.
2. Look at the dark clouds! It ____ (rain).
3. I think she ____ (be) a doctor in the future.
4. They ____ (have) a party this weekend.
5. He ____ (not, come) to the meeting tomorrow.
6. ____ you ____ (help) me with my homework?

#### 二、选择题

1. I think it ____ rain tomorrow.
   A. will B. is going to C. is D. was

2. She ____ a new job next month.
   A. start B. starts C. is going to start D. starting

3. ____ you ____ to the party tonight?
   A. Are, going B. Do, go C. Will, go D. Are, go

4. The train ____ in five minutes.
   A. arrives B. is arriving C. arrive D. arrived

5. I ____ you later. I have to finish this work first.
   A. call B. will call C. am calling D. called

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. I will go to school tomorrow. （改为否定句）
   _________________________________________________
2. She is going to buy a new car. （改为一般疑问句）
   _________________________________________________
3. They will have a meeting next week. （用 this weekend 改写）
   _________________________________________________
4. He is going to be a teacher. （对画线部分提问）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. will visit / am going to visit
2. is going to rain（根据证据预测）
3. will be
4. are going to have
5. isn't going to come / won't come
6. Will, help

**二、**
1. A（think 后接将来时，表预测）
2. C（next month 将来的计划，用 be going to）
3. A（be going to 的疑问句）
4. B（进行时表将来，in five minutes）
5. B（later 将来时间，用 will）

**三、**
1. I won't go to school tomorrow. / I am not going to school tomorrow.
2. Is she going to buy a new car?
3. They are going to have a meeting this weekend.
4. What is he going to be?
