# 一般现在时

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 08 |
| 语法点 | 一般现在时 |
| 核心内容 | 事实、习惯、状态 |

## 常用词组/搭配

always, often, every day, usually

## 详细笔记

### 一、定义与作用

- **定义**：表示经常发生、习惯性动作或客观事实的时态
- **作用**：描述现在的情况、习惯、自然规律等

### 二、构成

#### 1. 主语 + 动词原形（主语是第一人称/第二人称/复数）
- I **work** every day.
- You **play** tennis well.
- They **go** to school on foot.

#### 2. 主语 + 动词第三人称单数（主语是第三人称单数）
- He **works** every day.
- She **likes** music.
- The cat **eats** fish.

### 三、动词第三人称单数变化规则

| 规则 | 例词 |
|------|------|
| 一般加 -s | works, likes, eats |
| 以 s, x, z, ch, sh 结尾加 -es | watches, teaches, finishes |
| 以辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i + -es | studies, carries, flies |
| 以元音字母 + y 结尾，直接加 -s | plays, stays |
| 以 o 结尾，加 -es | goes, does, watches |

### 四、时间状语

| 频率副词 | 例句 |
|----------|------|
| always（总是） | She is always happy. |
| usually（通常） | I usually get up at 7. |
| often（经常） | He often reads books. |
| sometimes（有时） | We sometimes watch TV. |
| seldom（很少） | She seldom complains. |
| never（从不） | I never smoke. |

| 时间短语 | 例句 |
|----------|------|
| every day/week/year | I exercise every day. |
| on weekdays | I go to school on weekdays. |
| in the morning/afternoon | She studies in the morning. |

### 五、be 动词的一般现在时

- **肯定句**：主语 + am/is/are
  - I **am** a student.
  - He **is** tall.
  - They **are** happy.

- **否定句**：主语 + am/is/are + not
  - I **am not** a teacher.
  - He **is not** here.
  - They **are not** busy.

- **疑问句**：Am/Is/Are + 主语...?
  - **Are** you a student?
  - **Is** he at home?
  - **Are** they ready?

### 例句

#### 描述习惯/日常活动
1. I **get up** at 6:30 every morning.
2. She **brushes** her teeth twice a day.
3. They **play** football after school.
4. He **works** in a hospital.

#### 描述客观事实
1. The sun **rises** in the east.
2. Water **boils** at 100℃.
3. Birds **fly** in the sky.
4. The earth **goes** around the sun.

#### 描述状态
1. He **is** a good student.
2. She **looks** happy.
3. They **are** at home.
4. This book **is** interesting.

#### 频率副词的使用
1. I **always** go to bed at 10 o'clock.
2. She **usually** has breakfast at 7.
3. He **often** watches movies on weekends.
4. We **sometimes** eat out.
5. They **never** complain about work.

### 注意事项

1. **第三人称单数是易错点**：当主语是 he/she/it 或单数名词时，谓语动词要加 -s 或 -es。
   - 错误：He like apples.
   - 正确：He likes apples.

2. **be 动词和行为动词不能同时使用**：
   - 错误：He is goes to school.
   - 正确：He goes to school. / He is a student.

3. **频率副词的位置**：通常放在行为动词之前，be 动词之后。
   - She always works late.（行为动词前）
   - She is always happy.（be 动词后）

4. **every day vs. every day 的区别**：
   - every day（分开）：每天（作状语）
   - everyday（合在一起）：每天的（作形容词，如 everyday English）

5. **一般现在时也表将来**：用于时刻表、航班、火车等固定时刻表。
   - The train leaves at 8:00.
   - The movie starts at 7:30.

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给动词的适当形式填空

1. She ____ (watch) TV every evening.
2. He ____ (work) in a bank.
3. My mother ____ (cook) delicious food.
4. The cat ____ (eat) fish.
5. I usually ____ (go) to school on foot.
6. She always ____ (be) happy.
7. They ____ (be) students.
8. He ____ (not like) math.

#### 二、选择题

1. He ____ English every day.
   A. study B. studies C. studying D. studied

2. She ____ a teacher.
   A. is B. are C. am D. be

3. I ____ breakfast at 7:00 every morning.
   A. have B. has C. had D. having

4. The sun ____ in the east.
   A. rise B. rises C. rising D. rose

5. She ____ late for school.
   A. never is B. is never C. never be D. is not never

#### 三、按要求完成句子

1. He plays football every day. （改为否定句）
   _________________________________________________
2. She is a teacher. （改为一般疑问句）
   _________________________________________________
3. They go to school on foot. （改为第三人称单数）
   _________________________________________________
4. I often read books. （用 always 改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. watches    2. works    3. cooks    4. eats    5. go    6. is    7. are    8. doesn't like

**二、**
1. B（第三人称单数加 -es）
2. A（she 用 is）
3. A（I 用动词原形）
4. B（客观事实用一般现在时）
5. B（频率副词在 be 动词后）

**三、**
1. He doesn't play football every day.
2. Is she a teacher?
3. He goes to school on foot.
4. I always read books.
