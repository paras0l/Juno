# 强调句

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 37 |
| 语法点 | 强调句 |
| 核心内容 | It is/was...that/who... |

## 常用词组/搭配

do/does/did + 动词原形

## 详细笔记

### 一、强调句概述

- **定义**：通过特定结构突出句子的某个成分
- **方式**：It is/was...that/who + 被强调部分

### 二、It is/was... that/who 强调句

#### 1. 基本结构
- **强调主语（人）**：It is/was + 主语 + that/who + 其余部分
- **强调宾语（人）**：It is/was + 宾语 + that/who + 其余部分
- **强调其他成分**：It is/was + 其他成分 + that + 其余部分

#### 2. 时态
- 现在时：It is...that...
- 过去时：It was...that...

#### 3. 例句
1. It is **he** that/who helped me. （强调主语）
2. It is **English** that she teaches. （强调宾语）
3. It was **yesterday** that I met her. （强调时间状语）
4. It was **in Beijing** that I met her. （强调地点状语）

### 三、强调句的疑问句

#### 1. 一般疑问句
- Was it in 1999 that he went to America?
- Is it you that are looking for?

#### 2. 特殊疑问句
- When was it that you called me?
- Where was it that you lost your watch?
- Who was it that helped you?

### 四、not...until 的强调

- **结构**：It is/was not until + 时间 + that + 主句
- He didn't leave until I came. → **It was not until** I came **that** he left.
- Not until I came did he leave. → **It was not until** I came **that** he left.

### 五、do/does/did + 动词原形（谓语动词的强调）

- **结构**：do/does/did + 动词原形
- **含义**：确实、真的、的确
- **例**：I **do** love you. （我真的爱你）
- **例**：She **does** study hard. （她确实学习努力）
- **例**：He **did** come yesterday. （他昨天的确来了）

### 六、强调句与定语从句/状语从句的区别

| 句型 | 特点 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 强调句 | 可还原为正常语序 | It is in Beijing that I met her. |
| 定语从句 | 有先行词 | This is the place where I met her. |
| 状语从句 | 无 that | It was because he was ill that he didn't come. |

### 七、强调句的还原

- 将 It is/was...that 去掉，剩余部分应是一个完整句子
- It is **because** he was ill **that** he didn't come. （because 引导原因状语从句）

### 例句

#### 强调主语
1. It is **he** that/who helped me yesterday.
2. It was **my mother** that called me just now.

#### 强调宾语
1. It is **English** that she teaches.
2. It was **him** that I saw at the supermarket.

#### 强调时间状语
1. It was **yesterday** that I met her.
2. It was **last year** that he went to America.

#### 强调地点状语
1. It was **in Beijing** that I met her.
2. It was **at the station** that I lost my bag.

#### 强调原因/方式
1. It was **because** he was ill **that** he didn't come.
2. It was **in this way** that he solved the problem.

#### do/does/did 强调
1. I **do** believe you.
2. She **does** work hard.
3. He **did** come to the party last night.

#### not...until 强调
1. It was not until **midnight** that he finished his work.
2. It was not until **I told him** that he understood.

### 注意事项

1. **强调句中 that/who 的选择**：
   - 强调人时可用 that 或 who
   - 强调物时只用 that
   - 强调主语时：It is he that/who...

2. **强调句不能强调谓语动词**：
   - 错误：It is works that he.
   - 正确：He does work. （用 do 强调）

3. **强调句与定语从句的区别**：
   - 强调句去掉 It is/was...that 后句子仍完整
   - 定语从句有先行词

4. **not...until 强调结构**：
   - 正常句：He didn't leave until I came.
   - 强调句：It was not until I came that he left.

5. **do/does/did 强调只用于肯定句**。

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当形式填空

1. It was yesterday ____ I met her.
2. It is English ____ she teaches.
3. It was in Beijing ____ I met her.
4. It was because he was ill ____ he didn't come.
5. I ____ believe you. （真的相信）

#### 二、选择题

1. It was in the library ____ I met her.
   A. that B. where C. when D. what

2. It is you ____ are to blame.
   A. which B. that C. whom D. when

3. Not until he arrived home ____ realize the problem.
   A. did he B. he did C. he had D. had he

4. It was not until midnight ____ he finished his work.
   A. that B. when C. where D. what

5. He did come yesterday. （强调）
   A. 正确 B. 错误

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. I met her in Beijing yesterday. （用强调句改写）
   _________________________________________________
2. He didn't come because he was ill. （用强调句改写）
   _________________________________________________
3. I do believe you. （对 do 提问）
   _________________________________________________

   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. that    2. that    3. that    4. that    5. do

**二、**
1. A（强调句用 that）
2. B（强调人用 that/who）
