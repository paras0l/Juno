# 主谓一致

| 属性 | 内容 |

| 核心内容 | 就近原则、就远原则、集体名词 |

## 常用词组/搭配

as well as, together with, either...or, a number of

## 详细笔记

### 一、主谓一致定义

- **定义**：谓语动词必须与主语在人称和数上保持一致
- **原则**：语法一致、意义一致、就近原则

### 二、主谓一致的三大原则

#### 1. 语法一致
- 主语是单数，谓语用单数
- 主语是复数，谓语用复数
- The boy **plays** football. / The boys **play** football.

#### 2. 意义一致
- 主语形式是单数，但意义是复数，谓语用复数
- The police **are** looking for the criminal. （警察，复数意义）
- The family **are** all tall. （家人，复数意义）

#### 3. 就近原则
- 谓语与靠近的主语保持一致
- Either you or I **am** wrong.
- Neither the students nor the teacher **is** coming.

### 三、常见主谓一致情况

#### 1. 集体名词

| 集体名词 | 谓语用单数（整体） | 谓语用复数（成员） |
|----------|------------------|-------------------|
| family | The family is large. | The family are all present. |
| team | The team is strong. | The team are wearing different uniforms. |
| class | The class is big. | The class are doing different things. |
| audience | The audience is large. | The audience are clapping. |

#### 2. 复数名词作主语
- The shoes **are** under the bed.
- scissors, trousers, glasses, etc. （用复数）

#### 3. 不可数名词作主语
- The water **is** cold.
- The news **is** good.

#### 4. 名词+with/together with/as well as
- 谓语与名词一致，不受介词短语影响
- My father, **as well as** I, **is** going to Beijing.
- The teacher, **together with** his students, **is** visiting the museum.

### 四、就远原则（复数名词/代词）

- as well as, along with, together with, besides, except, but, with
- 谓语与主语一致，不受这些词影响
- Everyone **except** me **is** going. （except me 修饰 everyone）

### 五、数词/量词作主语

| 结构 | 谓语 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| a number of | 复数 | A number of students **are** here. |
| the number of | 单数 | The number of students **is** 50. |
| a lot of | 单复数均可 | A lot of money **is** spent. / A lot of people **are** here. |
| plenty of | 单复数均可 | Plenty of water **is** needed. / Plenty of people **are** here. |
| most of | 单复数均可 | Most of the time **is** spent on reading. |
| half of | 单复数均可 | Half of the apple **is** eaten. / Half of the apples **are** eaten. |

### 六、定语从句中的主谓一致

- One of + 复数名词 + 定语从句：从句谓语用复数
- The only one of + 复数名词 + 定语从句：从句谓语用单数

- He is one of the students who **pass** the exam. （复数）
- He is the only one of the students who **passes** the exam. （单数）

### 七、特殊主谓一致

| 主语 | 谓语 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| either...or | 就近 | Either you or I **am** wrong. |
| neither...nor | 就近 | Neither he nor I **am** wrong. |
| not only...but also | 就近 | Not only the students but also the teacher **is** coming. |
| there be | 就近 | There **is** a book and two pens. |
| all | 单数/复数 | All **is** well. （指事）All **are** here. （指人） |
| none | 单数/复数 | None of us **is** perfect. / None of them **are** perfect. |

### 例句

#### 语法一致
1. The boy **plays** football every day.
2. The students **are** very happy.
3. She **likes** reading books.

#### 意义一致
1. The police **are** searching for the thief.
2. The family **are** all at home today.
3. The audience **are** clapping loudly.

#### 就近原则
1. Either you or I **am** wrong.
2. Neither he nor she **is** available.
3. Not only the teachers but also the student **is** happy.

#### 集体名词
1. The team **is** the best in the country. （整体）
2. The team **are** wearing different uniforms. （成员）

#### 名词+介词短语
1. My father, as well as my mother, **is** coming.
2. The teacher, together with his students, **is** visiting the museum.

#### 数词/量词
1. A number of students **are** late.
2. The number of students **is** increasing.
3. Half of the apple **is** eaten.

#### 定语从句
1. He is one of the students who **work** hard.
2. She is the only one of the students who **passes** the exam.

### 注意事项

1. **a number of vs. the number of**：
   - a number of：许多（复数动词）
   - the number of：……的数量（单数动词）

2. **集体名词的单复数**：
   - 指整体时用单数，指成员时用复数
   - My family is large. （家庭）
   - My family are all at home. （家人）

3. **with/together with/as well as 后主谓一致**：
   - 谓语与主语一致，不受介词短语影响
   - He, together with his friends, is going.

4. **one of 结构**：
   - one of + 复数名词：定语从句动词用复数
   - the only one of + 复数名词：定语从句动词用单数

### 练习题

#### 一、选择题

1. The number of students in our class ____ 50.
   A. is B. are C. were D. be

2. A number of students ____ late this morning.
   A. is B. are C. was D. be

3. Not only the teacher but also the students ____ happy.
   A. is B. are C. was D. be

4. Either you or he ____ wrong.
   A. am B. is C. are D. be

5. The police ____ looking for the criminal.
   A. is B. are C. was D. be

#### 二、用适当动词形式填空

1. My family ____ (be) all at home today.
2. The team ____ (play) very well.
3. He is one of the students who ____ (work) hard.
4. She is the only one of the students who ____ (pass) the exam.
5. Half of the apple ____ (be) eaten.

#### 三、按要求完成句子

1. A lot of people ____ (be) at the party. （填空）
2. The family, together with their friends, ____ (go) to the park. （用正确形式填空）
3. The news ____ (be) good. （填空）
4. Neither he nor I ____ (be) wrong. （填空）
5. ____ of the students is absent. （None / The number）

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. A（the number of + 单数名词，单数动词）
2. B（a number of + 复数名词，复数动词）
3. B（not only...but also 就近原则）
4. B（either...or 就近原则）
5. B（police 复数意义）

**二、**
1. are（family 指成员，复数）
2. is/plays（team 指整体，单数）
3. work（one of + 复数名词）
4. passes（the only one of + 复数名词）
5. is（half of + 不可数名词）

**三、**
1. are
2. goes
3. is
4. am
5. None
