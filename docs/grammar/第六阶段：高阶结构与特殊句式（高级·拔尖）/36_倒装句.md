# 倒装句

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 36 |
| 语法点 | 倒装句 |
| 核心内容 | 全部倒装与部分倒装 |

## 常用词组/搭配

Not only..., Hardly...when, Only then..., So...that

## 详细笔记

### 一、倒装句概述

- **定义**：将句子中的谓语动词或助动词移至主语之前
- **分类**：全部倒装、部分倒装

### 二、全部倒装

#### 1. 定义
- 将整个谓语动词移至主语之前
- 主语和谓语位置完全颠倒

#### 2. 常见情况

| 情况 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 地点状语提前 | 介词短语 + 动词 + 主语 | **In the room** is a table. |
| 方向副词提前 | 副词 + 动词 + 主语 | **Out** ran the boy. |
| There/Here/Then 开头 | There/Here/Then + 动词 + 主语 | **Here comes** the bus. |

#### 3. 注意事项
- 主语是代词时，不倒装：Here he comes. （不是 Here comes he.）
- 谓语动词通常是 be/come/go/stand/lie 等

### 三、部分倒装

#### 1. 定义
- 只将助动词/情态动词移至主语之前
- 主语和部分谓语位置颠倒

#### 2. 常见情况

##### (1) 否定词提前（须部分倒装）
| 否定词 | 例句 |
|--------|------|
| never | **Never** have I seen such a thing. |
| seldom | **Seldom** does he come late. |
| rarely | **Rarely** do we go to the cinema. |
| little | **Little** does he know about it. |
| not until | **Not until** did he finish his work did he leave. |
| not only | **Not only** is he a teacher but also a writer. |
| no sooner | **No sooner** had he arrived than it started to rain. |
| hardly/scarcely | **Hardly** had I reached home when the phone rang. |

##### (2) only 提前
- **Only** then did I understand the truth.
- **Only** in this way can we solve the problem.
- **Only** when I saw him did I realize the mistake.

##### (3) so/such...that 结构
- **So** happy was he **that** he danced.
- **Such** was the weather **that** we had to stay inside.

##### (4) 省略 if 的虚拟条件句
- **Were** I you, I would go. （= If I were you...）
- **Had** he come, he would have helped. （= If he had come...）
- **Should** it rain, we would cancel. （= If it should rain...）

### 四、常见倒装句型

| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| so + 形容词 | So + 形容词 + be + 主语 + that... | So angry was he that he couldn't speak. |
| such + 名词 | Such + 名词 + be + 主语 + that... | Such was his courage that everyone admired him. |
| neither/nor | Neither/Nor + 助动词 + 主语 | He doesn't like it. Neither do I. |
| only + 状语 | Only + 状语 + 助动词 + 主语 | Only then did I understand. |
| not only...but also | Not only + 助动词 + 主语 + 动词...but also... | Not only does he speak English but also French. |

### 例句

#### 全部倒装
1. **In the room** is a big table.
2. **Here comes** the bus.
3. **There lies** a book on the desk.
4. **Out** ran the children.
5. **Down** fell the leaves.

#### 部分倒装
1. **Never** have I seen such a beautiful place.
2. **Seldom** does he come late.
3. **Not until** I finished my work did I leave.
4. **Only then** did I understand.
5. **Only in this way** can we solve the problem.
6. **Not only** is he a teacher **but also** a writer.
7. **So** happy was he that he danced.
8. **Such** was the weather that we stayed inside.

#### 省略 if 的虚拟语气倒装
1. **Were** I you, I would go there.
2. **Had** I known the answer, I would have told you.
3. **Should** it rain, we would stay at home.

#### 其他倒装
1. I don't like it. **Neither/Nor do** I.
2. He can speak English. **So can** I.
3. **Hardly** had I got home when it started to rain.
4. **No sooner** had she arrived than the phone rang.

### 注意事项

1. **全部倒装中主语是代词时不倒装**：
   - 正确：Here he comes.
   - 错误：Here comes he.

2. **否定词提前必须部分倒装**：
   - 错误：Not until he came did I know.
   - 正确：Not until he came did I know. （已倒装）

3. **only 提前要部分倒装**：
   - 错误：Only he can understand.
   - 正确：Only then can he understand.

4. **not only...but also 句型**：
   - not only 放句首时，其后用部分倒装
   - Not only did he come late but also he forgot his homework.

5. **hardly/scarcely...when, no sooner...than**：
   - 主句用过去完成时，when/than 后用一般过去时

### 练习题

#### 一、按要求完成句子

1. ____ (Never) I have seen such a beautiful view.
2. ____ (Only then) I realized the truth.
3. ____ (Not only) he is a teacher ____ (but also) a writer.
4. Here ____ (come) the bus.
5. So tired ____ (be) she that she fell asleep.

#### 二、选择题

1. ____ came he home.
   A. Not until B. Not only C. Only when D. Never

2. Only in this way ____ solve the problem.
   A. can we B. we can C. we could D. could we

3. Not only ____ hard but also he works carefully.
   A. does he study B. he studies C. he study D. study he

4. ____ I were you, I would try again.
   A. If B. Were C. Should D. Had

5. Hardly ____ the house when it started to rain.
   A. I left B. did I leave C. I had left D. had I left
