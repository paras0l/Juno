# 独立主格结构

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 40 |
| 语法点 | 独立主格结构 |
| 核心内容 | 名词/代词+分词/形容词/介词短语 |

## 常用词组/搭配

Weather permitting, All things considered

## 详细笔记

### 一、独立主格结构定义

- **定义**：在句子中作状语，但带有自己的主语的结构
- **结构**：名词/代词 + 非谓语动词/形容词/介词短语
- **特点**：独立主格有自己的主语，与主句主语不同

### 二、独立主格的构成

#### 1. 名词/代词 + 现在分词
- Weather **permitting**, we will go out. （天气允许的话）
- The work **being done**, we went home. （工作完成后）
- Night **coming on**, we went home. （夜幕降临）

#### 2. 名词/代词 + 过去分词
- The work **finished**, we went home. （工作完成后）
- The letter **written**, he went to post it. （写完信后）
- The meeting **over**, we left the office. （会议结束后）

#### 3. 名词/代词 + 形容词
- He **absent**, the meeting couldn't start. （他缺席）
- The weather **fine**, we went out. （天气好）

#### 4. 名词/代词 + 介词短语
- He **in the room**, we couldn't work. （他在房间里）
- The teacher **with a book**, the class began. （老师拿着书）

#### 5. 名词/代词 + 副词
- He **away**, we missed him. （他不在）

### 三、独立主格的作用

| 作用 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 表时间 | 相当于时间状语从句 | The work **done**, we went home. |
| 表原因 | 相当于原因状语从句 | The weather **fine**, we went out. |
| 表条件 | 相当于条件状语从句 | Weather **permitting**, we will go. |
| 表伴随 | 伴随主句动作 | He lay on the grass, his eyes **looking** at the sky. |

### 四、独立主格与 with 的复合结构

#### with + 宾语 + 补语

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| with + 名词 + 现在分词 | He sat there, **with his eyes looking** at the sky. |
| with + 名词 + 过去分词 | The work **with it finished**, we left. |
| with + 名词 + 形容词 | She walked out, **with her head high**. |
| with + 名词 + 介词短语 | The man **with a bag** in his hand came in. |
| with + 名词 + 副词 | He slept, **with the light on**. |

### 五、独立主格的否定形式

- 否定词 not 放在前面
- **Not** finished the work, we couldn't leave.
- **Not** knowing the answer, I kept silent.

### 六、独立主格的独立主格 vs. 状语从句

- 独立主格：名词/代词 + 非谓语（主语不同）
- 状语从句：主语 + 谓语（主语相同可省略）
- Weather **permitting**, we will go. （独立主格）
- If weather **permits**, we will go. （条件状语从句）

### 例句

#### 名词+现在分词
1. Weather **permitting**, we will go out tomorrow.
2. The work **being done**, we went home.
3. Night **coming on**, the streets became quiet.

#### 名词+过去分词
1. The work **finished**, we went home.
2. The letter **written**, he went to post it.
3. The meeting **over**, we left the office.

#### 名词+形容词
1. He **absent**, the meeting couldn't start.
2. The weather **fine**, we went out.
3. She **still**, I couldn't hear anything.

#### 名词+介词短语
1. He **in the room**, we couldn't work quietly.
2. The teacher **with a book**, the class began.
3. She **with her hands full**, couldn't answer the phone.

#### with 结构
1. He sat there, **with his eyes looking** at the sky.
2. The work **with it finished**, we left the office.
3. She walked out, **with her head high**.
4. The man **with a bag in his hand** came in.
5. He slept, **with the light on**.

#### 否定形式
1. **Not** finished the work, we couldn't leave.
2. **Not** knowing the answer, I kept silent.
3. **Not** having received her letter, I couldn't reply.

### 注意事项

1. **独立主格与主句主语不同**：如果主语相同，应用状语从句的省略形式。
   - 正确：He came in, **smiling**. （独立主格，主语不同）
   - 正确：When **he came in**, he was smiling. （状语从句）

2. **现在分词 vs. 过去分词**：
   - 现在分词：与逻辑主语是主动关系
   - 过去分词：与逻辑主语是被动关系

3. **with 结构可转换为独立主格**：
   - With the work finished → The work finished

4. **独立主格没有谓语动词**，不是完整的句子。

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的独立主格填空

1. Weather ____ (permit), we will go out.
2. The work ____ (finish), we went home.
3. He sat there, his eyes ____ (look) at the sky.
4. The teacher ____ (with a book) came into the classroom.
5. ____ (not know) the answer, I kept silent.

#### 二、选择题

1. The work ____, we went home.
   A. finish B. finished C. finishing D. to finish

2. He came in, ____.
   A. smile B. smiled C. smiling D. smiles

3. With the work ____, we left.
   A. finish B. finished C. finishing D. to finish

4. ____ the weather fine, we went out.
   A. Be B. Being C. Been D. To be

5. He lay on the grass, his eyes ____ at the sky.
   A. look B. looked C. looking D. to look

#### 三、按要求改写句子

1. Because the weather was fine, we went out. （用独立主格改写）
   _________________________________________________
2. He finished his work. Then he went home. （用独立主格合并）
   _________________________________________________
3. The work was finished, so we went home. （用独立主格改写）
   _________________________________________________
4. Because he didn't know the answer, he kept silent. （用独立主格改写）
   _________________________________________________

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. permitting    2. finished    3. looking    4. with a book    5. Not knowing

**二、**
1. B（过去分词表被动）
2. C（现在分词表主动进行）
3. B（with + 名词 + 过去分词）
4. B（being 表主动）
5. C（独立主格，eyes 与 look 是主动关系）

**三、**
1. The weather being fine, we went out. / Weather permitting, we went out.
2. The work finished, he went home.
3. The work finished, we went home.
4. Not knowing the answer, he kept silent.
