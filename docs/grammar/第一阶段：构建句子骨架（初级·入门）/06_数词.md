## 详细笔记

### 一、基数词（Cardinal Numbers）

#### 1-12（需单独记忆）
one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten, eleven, twelve

#### 13-19（以 -teen 结尾）
thirteen, fourteen, fifteen, sixteen, seventeen, eighteen, nineteen

#### 20-99（十位数 + 个位数）
- 20: twenty
- 30: thirty
- 40: forty
- 50: fifty
- 60: sixty
- 70: seventy
- 80: eighty
- 90: ninety
- 例：21 → twenty-one, 99 → ninety-nine

#### 100 以上
- 100: one hundred
- 1,000: one thousand
- 1,000,000: one million
- 例：365 → three hundred and sixty-five

### 二、序数词（Ordinal Numbers）

|------|------|------|
| one | first | 1st |
| two | second | 2nd |

#### 20 以上（基数 + th）
- 20th: twentieth
- 21st: twenty-first
- 30th: thirtieth
- 100th: hundredth
- 1000th: thousandth

### 三、时间表达法

#### 整点
- 7:00 → seven o'clock / seven
- 8:00 → eight o'clock / eight

#### 几点半
- 7:30 → half past seven / seven thirty
- 8:15 → a quarter past eight / eight fifteen

#### 几点几十分
- 7:10 → ten past seven / seven ten
- 8:45 → a quarter to nine / eight forty-five

#### 常见时间表达
- in the morning/afternoon/evening
- at dawn/noon/night/midnight

### 四、日期表达法

#### 英式：日 + 月 + 年
- 2024年5月1日 → 1st May, 2024 / 1 May 2024

#### 美式：月 + 日 + 年
- 2024年5月1日 → May 1st, 2024 / May 1, 2024

#### 序数词的读法
- 读作：the first of May / May the first

### 五、年龄表达

- 表达年龄：in one's + 基数词复数
- in one's twenties（二十多岁）
- in one's thirties（三十多岁）
- in one's forties（四十多岁）

### 例句

#### 基数词
1. I have **three** brothers and **two** sisters.
2. There are **hundreds of** people in the square.
3. The book costs **ninety-nine** dollars.
4. **One thousand** students attended the meeting.

#### 序数词
1. Today is **the first** of January.
2. She is always **the first** to arrive.
3. This is **the third** time I met him.
4. He lives on the **twelfth** floor.

#### 时间表达
1. It is **seven o'clock**. / It is **seven**.
2. It is **half past eight**. / It is **eight thirty**.
3. It is **a quarter past nine**. / It is **nine fifteen**.
4. It is **a quarter to ten**. / It is **nine forty-five**.

#### 日期表达
1. **May 1st** is Labor Day. / **The first of May** is Labor Day.
2. My birthday is **November 22nd**.
3. **October 1st** is National Day of China.

#### 年龄表达
1. She is **in her twenties**.
2. He retired **in his sixties**.
3. The old man is **in his eighties**.

### 注意事项

1. **序数词前通常加 the**，但表示"又一个"时可用 a/an：
   - the first book（第一本书）
   - a second chance（又一次机会）

2. **hundreds/thousands/millions of 前不能加具体数字**，但可以加 several：
   - 错误：three hundreds of students
   - 正确：three hundred students / hundreds of students

3. **年份的读法**：
   - 1999 → nineteen ninety-nine
   - 2000 → two thousand
   - 2024 → twenty twenty-four

4. ** dozen（12）和 score（20）的用法**：
   - three dozen eggs（一打鸡蛋）
   - a score of people（二十个人）

5. **时间表达两种方式都可使用**：
   - 7:10 → ten past seven = seven ten
   - 7:50 → ten to eight = seven fifty

6. **基数词修饰名词时，单数形式不变**：
   - 错误：three books
   - 正确：three books（books 本身是复数）

### 练习题

#### 一、用英文写出下列数字

1. 23 — _____________
2. 45 — _____________
3. 99 — _____________
4. 156 — _____________
5. 1,000 — _____________

#### 二、用序数词填空

1. Today is _____________ (3) of May.
2. He is in the _____________ (10) grade.
3. This is my _____________ (1) time to visit Beijing.
4. The _____________ (21) student won the prize.
5. December is the _____________ (12) month of the year.

#### 三、选择题

1. There are ______ students in the school.
   A. hundred of B. hundreds of C. hundred D. hundreds

2. She got married ______.
   A. in her twenties B. in her twentieth C. in twenties D. in twenty

3. It is ______ now.
   A. half past six B. a half past six C. six half past D. half to six

4. ______ is Children's Day.
   A. June one B. One June C. June first D. First June

5. I need ______ eggs.
   A. three dozen B. three dozens C. three dozen of D. three dozens of

#### 四、用英语表达下列时间

1. 7:30 — _____________
2. 9:15 — _____________
3. 3:45 — _____________
4. 12:00 — _____________

#### 五、答案

**一、**
1. twenty-three
2. forty-five
3. ninety-nine
4. one hundred and fifty-six
5. one thousand

**二、**
1. third
2. tenth
3. first
4. twenty-first
5. twelfth

**三、**
1. B（hundreds of 表约数）
2. A（in one's twenties 表二十多岁）
3. A（half past six = six thirty）
4. C（June first = 6月1日）
5. A（three dozen 后直接加名词）

**四、**
1. half past seven / seven thirty
2. a quarter past nine / nine fifteen
3. a quarter to four / three forty-five
4. twelve o'clock / noon
