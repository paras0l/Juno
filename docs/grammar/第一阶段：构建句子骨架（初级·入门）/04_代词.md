# 代词

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 04 |
| 语法点 | 代词 |
| 核心内容 | 主格/宾格/形代/名代/反身代词 |

## 常用词组/搭配

each other, one another, by oneself

## 详细笔记

### 一、代词的分类

#### 1. 人称代词（Personal Pronoun）

| 主格 | 宾格 |
|------|------|
| I | me |
| you | you |
| he | him |
| she | her |
| it | it |
| we | us |
| they | them |

- **主格**：作主语
  - **例**：She is a teacher. / They are students.
- **宾格**：作宾语
  - **例**：I like him. / We help her.

#### 2. 物主代词（Possessive Pronoun）

| 形容词性物主代词 | 名词性物主代词 |
|------------------|----------------|
| my | mine |
| your | yours |
| his | his |
| her | hers |
| its | its |
| our | ours |
| their | theirs |

- **形容词性物主代词**：后接名词（作定语）
  - **例**：my book, her mother
- **名词性物主代词**：独立使用（作主语、宾语、表语）
  - **例**：That book is **mine**. / This is **hers**.

#### 3. 指示代词（Demonstrative Pronoun）
- **this/these** — 这、这些（指近处）
- **that/those** — 那、那些（指远处）
- **such** — 这样的
- **same** — 同样的

#### 4. 反身代词（Reflexive Pronoun）

| 单数 | 复数 |
|------|------|
| myself | ourselves |
| yourself | yourselves |
| himself/herself/itself | themselves |

- **用法**：作宾语、表语，或用于强调
- **例**：He hurt **himself**. / She is proud of **herself**. / I did it **myself**.

#### 5. 不定代词（Indefinite Pronoun）

| 类别 | 词 |
|------|-----|
| 肯定 | some, something, someone, somebody, one |
| 否定 | no, nothing, nobody, none, no one |
| 疑问 | what, which, who, whom, whose |
| 复合 | anything, anyone, everybody, everyone, everywhere |

- **some** — 用于肯定句/请求/建议
  - 例：There is **some** water.
- **any** — 用于否定句/疑问句
  - 例：Is there **any** water?
- **many/much** — 许多（后接可数/不可数）
  - 例：many books, much money
- **a lot of / lots of** — 大量
- **few/little** — 几乎没有（否定含义）
- **a few/a little** — 一点（肯定含义）

### 二、相互代词

- **each other** — 互相（两者之间）
- **one another** — 互相（两者以上）
- **例**：We help **each other**. / They love **one another**.

### 例句

#### 人称代词
1. **She** is my sister. （主格作主语）
2. I saw **him** yesterday. （宾格作宾语）
3. **They** are playing in the park. （主格）
4. Please help **us**. （宾格）

#### 物主代词
1. This is **my** book. （形容词性，后接名词）
2. The book is **mine**. （名词性，独立使用）
3. **Our** teacher is very kind. （形容词性物主代词）
4. This house is **theirs**. （名词性物主代词）

#### 指示代词
1. **This** is my pen. / **These** are my books.
2. **That** is a beautiful flower. / **Those** are old trees.
3. I don't like **such** weather.
4. We are in the **same** situation.

#### 反身代词
1. He hurt **himself** while playing.
2. She introduced **herself** to everyone.
3. You should take care of **yourself**.
4. I finished the work **myself**.

#### 不定代词
1. There is **some** milk in the fridge.
2. Is there **anyone** at home?
3. **Few** people understand this.
4. I have **a little** money with me.
5. **Many** students passed the exam.

### 注意事项

1. **人称代词的主格和宾格不能混用**：
   - 主语用主格：She is a teacher.
   - 宾语用宾格：I saw her yesterday.

2. **形容词性物主代词后必须接名词**，名词性物主代词独立使用：
   - 错误：This is mine book.
   - 正确：This is my book. / This book is mine.

3. **this/these 指近处，that/those 指远处**：
   - This is my house. （近处）
   - That is her house. （远处）

4. **some 和 any 的用法**：
   - some 用于肯定句：I have some apples.
   - any 用于否定句/疑问句：I don't have any apples. / Do you have any apples?

5. **few/little 和 a few/a little 的区别**：
   - few/little = 几乎没有（否定）
   - a few/a little = 有一点（肯定）

6. **反身代词不能作主语**：
   - 错误：Myself made this.
   - 正确：I made this myself. / I myself made this.

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的代词填空

1. ______ (我) am a student.
2. This book is ______ (我的).
3. ______ (她) is playing with ______ (她的) cat.
4. We help ______ (互相).
5. I did it ______ (我自己).

#### 二、选择题

1. This is ______ book.
   A. my B. mine C. I D. me

2. The book is ______.
   A. my B. mine C. myself D. me

3. ______ students are in the classroom.
   A. Any B. Some C. No D. Much

4. Is there ______ water in the glass?
   A. some B. any C. a few D. many

5. He hurt ______ while playing football.
   A. himself B. his C. him D. herself

#### 三、用 some, any, much, many, few, little 填空

1. There are ______ books on the desk.
2. There isn't ______ water in the bottle.
3. I have ______ money but not ______ time.
4. ______ people came to the party.
5. There is ______ milk in the cup.

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. I    2. mine    3. She, her    4. each other    5. myself

**二、**
1. A（形容词性物主代词后接名词）
2. B（名词性物主代词独立使用）
3. B（some + 复数名词）
4. B（any 用于疑问句）
5. A（反身代词作宾语）

**三、**
1. some/many
2. any
3. much, much
4. Some/Many
5. some/little
