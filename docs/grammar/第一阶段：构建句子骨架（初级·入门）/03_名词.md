# 名词

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 03 |
| 语法点 | 名词 |
| 核心内容 | 可数/不可数、单复数、所有格 |

## 常用词组/搭配

a piece of, a lot of, plenty of

## 详细笔记

### 一、名词的分类

#### 1. 可数名词（Countable Noun）
- **特点**：可以用数词计数，有单数和复数形式
- **例词**：book → books, apple → apples, student → students

#### 2. 不可数名词（Uncountable Noun）
- **特点**：不能用数词直接计数，没有复数形式
- **分类**：
  - 物质名词：water, milk, bread, rice, gold
  - 抽象名词：happiness, love, time, freedom, music
  - 液体/气体：juice, tea, air, gas

### 二、名词的数

#### 可数名词复数变化规则

| 规则 | 例词 |
|------|------|
| 一般加 -s | book → books, dog → dogs |
| 以 s, x, z, ch, sh 结尾加 -es | class → classes, box → boxes |
| 以辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i + -es | baby → babies, city → cities |
| 以元音字母 + y 结尾，直接加 -s | boy → boys, day → days |
| 以 f/fe 结尾，变 f/fe 为 v + -es | knife → knives, leaf → leaves |
| 以 o 结尾，加 -es（无生命名词加 -s） | potato → potatoes, photo → photos |

#### 不规则复数

| 变化方式 | 例词 |
|----------|------|
| 内部元音变化 | man → men, woman → women, foot → feet, tooth → teeth |
| 单复数同形 | sheep, deer, fish, Chinese, Japanese |
| 变尾部 | child → children, mouse → mice, ox → oxen |

#### 不可数名词的计量

| 量词 | 用法 |
|------|------|
| a piece of | a piece of paper, a piece of bread |
| a cup of | a cup of coffee |
| a bottle of | a bottle of water |
| a bowl of | a bowl of rice |
| a slice of | a slice of meat |
| a drop of | a drop of oil |

### 三、名词所有格（Possessive Case）

#### 1. 's 所有格

- **有生命物体**：Tom's book, my mother's hair
- **时间/距离**：today's newspaper, ten minutes' walk
- **国家/城市**：China's population, Beijing's weather

#### 2. of 所有格

- **无生命物体**：the door of the room, the color of the flower
- **部分/所属**：a friend of mine, a picture of his

#### 3. 双重所有格

- **结构**：of + 's 或 of + 名词
- **例句**：a friend of my father's, that beautiful daughter of hers

### 例句

#### 可数与不可数
1. I have three **books**. （可数）
2. She drinks two cups of **coffee** every day. （不可数，用量词）
3. There is some **bread** on the table. （不可数）
4. We need **water**. （不可数）

#### 复数变化
1. There are five **children** in the playground.
2. The **sheep** are eating grass.
3. She has two **knives** in the kitchen.
4. There are many **tomatoes** in the basket.

#### 所有格
1. This is **Tom's** book. （'s 所有格）
2. The window **of the classroom** is broken. （of 所有格）
3. He is a friend **of my father's**. （双重所有格）
4. **Ten minutes'** walk from here is the station.

### 注意事项

1. **不可数名词不能用 a/an 修饰**，但可以用量词：
   - 错误：a water, an information
   - 正确：a cup of water, a piece of information

2. **有些名词既是可数又是不可数**（注意意思变化）：
   - **glass** — 玻璃（不可数）/ 玻璃杯（可数）
   - **paper** — 纸（不可数）/ 论文、试卷（可数）
   - **time** — 时间（不可数）/ 次数、倍数（可数）

3. **以 o 结尾的名词复数**：
   - 有生命加 -es：tomato, potato, hero, negro
   - 无生命加 -s：photo, piano, radio, logo

4. **复合名词的复数**：通常将主体名词变为复数：
   - mother-in-law → mothers-in-law
   - boy friend → boy friends

5. **所有格使用原则**：
   - 有生命物体一般用 's：Tom's, my mother's
   - 无生命物体一般用 of：the leg of the table
   - 但表示时间、距离、国家、城市时，即使无生命也用 's

### 练习题

#### 一、用所给名词的适当形式填空

1. There are three ______ (child) in the family.
2. I need two ______ (glass) of water.
3. She bought five ______ (tomato) yesterday.
4. The ______ (sheep) are eating grass in the field.
5. Here are some ______ (information) for you.

#### 二、选择题

1. There is ______ water in the bottle.
   A. a B. an C. some D. a few

2. He is a friend of ______.
   A. my father B. my father's C. my fathers D. father

3. The ______ shoes are very expensive.
   A. women's B. womens' C. womens D. woman

4. It takes me ten ______ walk to get to school.
   A. minute B. minutes' C. minutes D. minute's

5. Do you need ______ bread?
   A. any B. some C. a D. an

#### 三、用所给词的正确形式填空

1. There are many ______ (sheep) on the farm.
2. I'd like two ______ (cup) of coffee.
3. This is a ______ (Germany) car.
4. The ______ (child) are playing in the park.
5. It's about an ______ (hour) walk.

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. children    2. glasses    3. tomatoes    4. sheep    5. information

**二、**
1. C（some 修饰不可数名词）
2. B（双重所有格）
3. A（women's 复数所有格）
4. B（ten minutes' 表示时间所有格）
5. B（some 用于疑问句表请求）

**三、**
1. sheep（单复数同形）
2. cups
3. German（German 是形容词）
4. children（不规则复数）
5. hour's（an hour's walk 表示时间距离）
