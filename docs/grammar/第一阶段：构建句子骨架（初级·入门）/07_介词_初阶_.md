# 介词（初阶）

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 序号 | 07 |
| 语法点 | 介词（初阶） |
| 核心内容 | 时间介词(at/in/on)、地点介词 |

## 常用词组/搭配

at night, in the morning, on Monday

## 详细笔记

### 一、时间介词

#### at（具体时刻）
- at 7:00（7点）
- at noon（中午）
- at night（晚上）
- at midnight（午夜）
- at dawn/ sunrise（日出时）
- at the weekend（在周末，英式）

#### in（较长的时间）
- in the morning/afternoon/evening（在早上/下午/晚上）
- in January（在1月）
- in 2024（在2024年）
- in spring/summer/fall/winter（在春夏秋冬）
- in the past（在过去）

#### on（具体某一天）
- on Monday（在周一）
- on July 1st（在7月1日）
- on a cold morning（在寒冷的早晨）
- on the weekend（在周末，美式）

### 二、地点介词

#### at（具体地点）
- at school（在上学）
- at home（在家）
- at the bus stop（在公交站）
- at the door（在门口）

#### in（在……内部）
- in the room（在房间里）
- in the box（在盒子里）
- in China/Beijing（在中国/北京）

#### on（表面接触）
- on the table（在桌上）
- on the wall（在墙上）
- on the floor（在地板上）
- on the map（在地图上）

### 三、方向介词

| 介词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| to | 到……去 | go to school |
| from | 从……来 | come from China |
| towards | 朝……方向 | walk towards the door |
| into | 进入 | go into the room |
| out of | 从……出来 | go out of the door |
| up | 向上 | go up the stairs |
| down | 向下 | go down the hill |

### 四、方式介词

- by + 交通工具（无冠词）：by bus, by train, by plane, by car
- with + 工具/方式：write with a pen, help with a smile
- in + 语言/方式：in English, in a hurry

### 五、常见介词短语

| 介词短语 | 含义 |
|----------|------|
| at first | 首先 |
| at last | 最后 |
| at once | 立刻 |
| in fact | 实际上 |
| in time | 及时 |
| on time | 准时 |
| of course | 当然 |
| for example | 例如 |
| as for | 至于 |
| instead of | 代替 |

### 例句

#### 时间介词
1. I wake up **at** 6:00 every morning.
2. We have classes **in** the morning.
3. The meeting is **on** Monday.
4. She was born **in** 2010.
5. We usually go shopping **at** the weekend.

#### 地点介词
1. The book is **in** the bag.
2. There is a picture **on** the wall.
3. He is **at** the bus stop.
4. She lives **in** Shanghai.
5. The cat is **under** the table.

#### 方向介词
1. I go **to** school every day.
2. She comes **from** America.
3. He walked **towards** the window.
4. Please go **into** the room.
5. They ran **out of** the building.

#### 方式介词
1. I go to work **by** bus.
2. She writes **with** her right hand.
3. Can you say it **in** English?
4. He came **in** a hurry.

#### 介词短语
1. **At first**, I didn't understand the problem.
2. **In fact**, he is a good student.
3. Please arrive **on time**.
4. **Of course**, I will help you.

### 注意事项

1. **at/in/on 的时间用法区别**：
   - at + 具体时刻：at 7:00, at noon, at night
   - in + 较长的时间：in the morning, in 2024, in summer
   - on + 具体某一天：on Monday, on July 1st, on a cold morning

2. **at/in/on 的地点用法区别**：
   - at + 小地点（具体地址）：at the door, at the bus stop
   - in + 内部：in the room, in the box
   - on + 表面：on the table, on the wall

3. **by + 交通工具的固定用法**：
   - 交通工具前不加冠词：by bus, by car, by train
   - 特殊：on foot（步行）, in a car（在小汽车里）

4. **介词和动词的固定搭配**：
   - look at, listen to, wait for, ask for, come from
   - 这些搭配需要单独记忆

5. **on the weekend vs. at the weekend**：
   - 美式英语：on the weekend
   - 英式英语：at the weekend

### 练习题

#### 一、用适当的介词填空（at, in, on, by, with）

1. I usually get up ______ 6:00 ______ the morning.
2. The cat is sitting ______ the chair.
3. She goes to school ______ bus.
4. He was born ______ January, 2010.
5. There is a picture ______ the wall.
6. We will meet ______ Monday.
7. Write your name ______ a pen.
8. She lives ______ Shanghai.
9. The meeting starts ______ once.
10. He came home ______ a hurry.

#### 二、选择题

1. I often go to school ______ foot.
   A. by B. on C. in D. with

2. The train arrives ______ 3:00 ______ the afternoon.
   A. at, in B. in, on C. on, at D. at, on

3. There is a book ______ the table.
   A. in B. on C. at D. to

4. He comes ______ Canada.
   A. from B. to C. at D. in

5. Let's meet ______ the school gate.
   A. in B. on C. at D. to

#### 三、用适当的介词完成句子

1. She is good ______ math. （擅长）
2. I wait ______ the bus every morning. （等待）
3. Please look ______ the blackboard. （看）
4. He listens ______ music every day. （听）
5. Thank you ______ your help. （感谢）

#### 四、答案

**一、**
1. at, in
2. on
3. by
4. in
5. on
6. on
7. with
8. in
9. at
10. in

**二、**
1. B（on foot 步行）
2. A（at + 具体时间，in + 上午/下午/晚上）
3. B（在表面上用 on）
4. A（come from 来自）
5. C（at + 具体小地点）

**三、**
1. in（be good at 擅长）
2. for（wait for 等待）
3. at（look at 看）
4. to（listen to 听）
5. for（thank sb. for 因……感谢）
